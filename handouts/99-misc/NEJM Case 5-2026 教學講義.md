## 4. 討論 (Discussion)
4.1 Reninoma (Juxtaglomerular Cell Tumor) 腎素瘤
What is Reninoma? 什麼是腎素瘤？
Definition 定義:
• Rare benign tumor arising from juxtaglomerular cells of the kidney
• 源自腎臟腎絲球旁細胞的罕見良性腫瘤
• Also known as: Juxtaglomerular cell tumor (JGCT)
• First described by Robertson in 1967
Epidemiology 流行病學:
• Approximately 100-150 cases reported in literature
• Mean age: 20-30 years (range: 6-69 years)
• Slight female predominance
• Most cases are benign; rare reports of metastatic disease

Reninoma 與顱內高壓的關聯
本病例的獨特之處：病人同時有papilledema 和elevated intracranial pressure。 Proposed Mechanism 可能機制:
• Aldosterone stimulates mineralocorticoid receptors in choroid plexus epithelium
• Downstream effect: increased Na+-K+-ATPase synthesis
• Na+-K+-ATPase is a primary driver of CSF production
• Result: CSF overproduction →elevated ICP
雖然血清aldosterone 在本病例是正常的，hyperreninemia 可能創造了類似hyperaldos-
teronism 的環境，導致CSF 過度產生。 4.3 Clinical Manifestations 臨床表現
Classic Triad of Reninoma 腎素瘤經典三聯徵
1. Hypertension —often severe and refractory 高血壓，常為嚴重且難治
2. Hypokalemia —due to secondary hyperaldosteronism 低血鉀
3. Elevated plasma renin activity —massively elevated 血漿腎素活性極度升高
Other clinical features:
• Headache (common presenting symptom)
• Visual disturbances (if papilledema present)
• Polyuria, polydipsia (from hypokalemia)
• Muscle weakness (from hypokalemia)
• May present with hypertensive emergency

4.5 Management 處置
4.5.1 Medical Management 內科處置
目標：控制血壓和糾正低血鉀，作為手術前的橋接治療。 • ACE Inhibitors: Block angiotensin II production (captopril, lisinopril)
• ARBs: Block angiotensin II receptor (losartan)
• Direct Renin Inhibitors: Block renin directly (aliskiren)
• Spironolactone: Aldosterone antagonist + K+-sparing
• Calcium Channel Blockers: Nicardipine IV for acute control
• Alpha-blockers: Phentolamine (while pheochromocytoma ruled out)
> **重點提醒**
為什麼ACE inhibitors alone 效果不佳？
本病例中，即使使用maximal dose captopril→lisinopril，血壓仍難以控制。 原因：Angiotensin II 可經由非ACE 途徑產生（如chymase），因此需要：
• 合併ARB 使用（block AT1 receptor）
• 合併direct renin inhibitor（block 源頭）
• 最終治療：手術切除腫瘤
4.5.2 Surgical Management 外科處置
Definitive Treatment: Surgical Resection
Procedure: Robot-assisted laparoscopic partial nephrectomy
Rationale for Partial Nephrectomy:
• Nephron-sparing approach preserves renal functional reserve
• Decreases risk of new-onset chronic renal insuﬀiciency
• In young patients: may improve overall survival vs radical nephrectomy
• Most reninomas are small (<4 cm) and amenable to partial resection
This Patient’s Outcome:
• Renal artery clamp time: <20 minutes
• No intraoperative complications
• Discharged home on POD 1
• Aliskiren discontinued due to hypotension post-op
• Subsequently normotensive without any antihypertensive therapy!

Robot-Assisted Laparoscopic Partial Nephrectomy
Timing: 6 weeks after initial hospitalization (to allow medical BP optimization)
Procedure Details:
• Robot-assisted laparoscopic approach
• Tumor excision with maximal preservation of normal parenchyma
• Renal artery clamp time: <20 minutes
Outcome:
• No intraoperative complications
• Discharged POD 1
• Normotensive without medications post-operatively
## 7. 學習重點 (Key Takeaways)
> **重點提醒**
1. Secondary Hypertension Workup in Young Patients
• Hypertension in adolescents/young adults warrants investigation for secondary causes
• Key differential: renal parenchymal disease, renovascular disease, endocrine causes
• Severe hypokalemia in a hypertensive patient suggests mineralocorticoid excess
• 年輕高血壓患者合併嚴重低血鉀→需考慮次發性原因
> **重點提醒**
2. Distinguishing Hyperaldosteronism Types
• Primary hyperaldosteronism: ↑Aldosterone, ↓Renin (high A:R ratio)
• Secondary hyperaldosteronism: ↑Aldosterone, ↑Renin (normal or low A:R ratio)
• Reninoma: ↑↑↑Renin, normal-to-↑Aldosterone
• Renin level is the KEY distinguishing feature!
• 腎素水平是區分的關鍵！
> **重點提醒**
3. Reninoma: Classic Triad
• Hypertension (often severe, refractory)
• Hypokalemia (from secondary hyperaldosteronism)
• Markedly elevated plasma renin activity
• Usually young patients (<40 years)
• 經典三聯徵：高血壓+ 低血鉀+ 腎素極度升高
> **重點提醒**
4. Pathologic Features of Reninoma
• Histology: Small polygonal-to-spindle cells, hyalinized vessels
• IHC: CD34+, GATA3+, Smooth muscle actin+, Renin+ (if available)
• Electron microscopy: Rhomboid renin crystals (pathognomonic)
• Most are benign; rare metastatic cases reported
• 電子顯微鏡下的菱形腎素結晶具診斷價值
> **重點提醒**
5. Treatment Strategy
• Medical: ACEi + ARB + Spironolactone + Aliskiren (may need combination)
• Why ACEi alone insuﬀicient: Non-ACE pathways (chymase) produce Angiotensin II
• Definitive treatment: Surgical resection
• Partial nephrectomy preferred (nephron-sparing)
• 手術切除是根治性治療
> **重點提醒**
6. Reninoma and Intracranial Hypertension
• Hyperreninemia can cause a hyperaldosteronism-like state
• Aldosterone stimulates mineralocorticoid receptors in choroid plexus
• Results in increased CSF production →elevated ICP →papilledema
• May take time to resolve after tumor removal
• 腎素瘤可能導致顱內壓升高和視乳頭水腫

### 8.2 答案與解析 (Answers and Explanations)
Question 1. Answer: (D)
Explanation: Reninoma is characterized by markedly elevated plasma renin activity due
to autonomous renin secretion by the tumor. In contrast, primary hyperaldosteronism has
suppressed/low renin with elevated aldosterone (high aldosterone-to-renin ratio). This is the
key distinguishing feature between these two conditions. 解析：腎素瘤的特徵是因腫瘤自主分泌腎素導致血漿腎素活性顯著升高。相比之下，原發
性醛固酮過多症的腎素受抑制/偏低，醛固酮升高（高醛固酮/腎素比值）。這是區分這兩種疾
病的關鍵特徵。 Question 2. Answer: (C)
Explanation: U waves are classic ECG findings in severe hypokalemia. They are typically
seen in leads V2, V3, and V4. Other ECG changes in hypokalemia include ST depression, T
wave flattening, and prolonged QT interval. Peaked T waves and widened QRS are seen in
hyperkalemia. 解析：U 波是嚴重低血鉀的經典心電圖發現，通常見於V2、V3 和V4 導程。低血鉀的其
他心電圖變化包括ST 段壓低、T 波變平和QT 間期延長。高尖T 波和QRS 波群增寬見於
高血鉀。 Question 3. Answer: (B)
Explanation: Pheochromocytoma can present similarly to reninoma with severe episodic
hypertension, headaches, and hypokalemia (from catecholamine-induced kaliuresis). Plasma
metanephrines and normetanephrines are the screening tests for pheochromocytoma. Normal
results help rule out this diagnosis. 解析：嗜鉻細胞瘤可表現為類似腎素瘤的嚴重陣發性高血壓、頭痛和低血鉀（由兒茶酚胺
引起的鉀離子流失）。血漿metanephrine 和normetanephrine 是篩檢嗜鉻細胞瘤的檢查。結
果正常有助於排除此診斷。 Question 4. Answer: (B)
Explanation: While ACE inhibitors block the conversion of angiotensin I to angiotensin
II by ACE, angiotensin II can also be produced by alternative pathways, particularly chymase. In the setting of massively elevated renin (as in reninoma), these alternative pathways become
more significant, explaining why ACE inhibitors alone are often insuﬀicient. 解析：雖然ACE 抑制劑阻斷ACE 將血管張力素I 轉化為血管張力素II，但血管張力素
II 也可通過其他途徑產生，特別是chymase。在腎素大量升高的情況下（如腎素瘤），這些替
代途徑變得更重要，解釋了為什麼單獨使用ACE 抑制劑通常不夠。 Question 5. Answer: (C)
Explanation: Reninoma is characterized by positive staining for renin (most specific, but
often unavailable), CD34, GATA3, and smooth muscle actin. Electron microscopy showing
rhomboid renin crystals (renin protogranules) is pathognomonic. Chromogranin A is positive
in pheochromocytoma; HMB-45 in angiomyolipoma. 解析：腎素瘤的特徵是renin 染色陽性（最特異，但常不可用）、CD34、GATA3 和平滑肌
肌動蛋白。電子顯微鏡顯示菱形腎素結晶（腎素原顆粒）具有病理診斷意義。Chromogranin
A 在嗜鉻細胞瘤中陽性；HMB-45 在血管肌脂瘤中陽性。 Question 6. Answer: (B)
Explanation: Low plasma renin activity with elevated aldosterone indicates autonomous
aldosterone secretion that suppresses the renin-angiotensin system, consistent with primary
hyperaldosteronism (Conn’s syndrome). Reninoma has HIGH renin. Liddle’s syndrome has
LOW renin and LOW aldosterone. 解析：血漿腎素活性低伴醛固酮升高表示自主性醛固酮分泌抑制了腎素-血管張力素系統，
符合原發性醛固酮過多症（Conn 氏症候群）。腎素瘤的腎素是高的。Liddle 氏症候群的腎素
和醛固酮都低。 Question 7. Answer: (C)
Explanation: Surgical resection is the definitive treatment for reninoma. Partial nephrec-
tomy is preferred when feasible to preserve renal function, especially in young patients. Medical
management is used as a bridge to surgery but is not curative. After successful tumor removal,
patients typically become normotensive. 解析：手術切除是腎素瘤的根治性治療。在可行時優先選擇部分腎切除術以保留腎功能，
特別是年輕患者。藥物治療用作手術前的橋接但不能治癒。成功切除腫瘤後，患者通常血壓
正常化。 Question 8. Answer: (C)
Explanation: Studies have linked hyperaldosteronism to idiopathic intracranial hyper-
tension. Aldosterone stimulates mineralocorticoid receptors in the choroid plexus epithelium,
leading to increased Na+-K+-ATPase synthesis, which is a primary driver of CSF production. Hyperreninemia in reninoma creates a hyperaldosteronism-like environment. 解析：研究已將醛固酮過多症與原發性顱內高壓聯繫起來。醛固酮刺激脈絡叢上皮細胞的
礦物皮質素受體，導致Na+-K+-ATPase 合成增加，這是CSF 產生的主要驅動因素。腎素瘤
的高腎素血症創造了類似醛固酮過多症的環境。 Question 9. Answer: (B)
Explanation: Post-operative hypotension requiring discontinuation of antihypertensive
medications indicates successful removal of the renin-secreting tumor. With the source of
autonomous renin secretion removed, the renin-angiotensin-aldosterone system normalizes, and
blood pressure returns to normal without medications. 解析：術後低血壓需要停用降壓藥表示成功切除了分泌腎素的腫瘤。隨著自主腎素分泌來
源被切除，腎素-血管張力素-醛固酮系統正常化，血壓在不用藥物的情況下恢復正常。 Question 10. Answer: (D)
Explanation: Partial nephrectomy is preferred over radical nephrectomy when feasible
because it preserves renal functional reserve, which is especially important in young patients. Reninoma is rare (not common), typically presents in young patients (mean age 20-30), and
the majority are benign. Reninoma has HIGH renin (not low). 解析：在可行時，部分腎切除術優於根治性腎切除術，因為它保留腎功能儲備，這對年輕
患者尤其重要。腎素瘤罕見（不常見），通常在年輕患者中出現（平均年齡20-30 歲），大多
數是良性的。腎素瘤的腎素是高的（不是低的）。 9 總結(Summary)
本病例的關鍵學習要點
1. Reninoma 的診斷思路：年輕患者的嚴重高血壓+ 低血鉀+ 腎臟腫塊→考慮腎
素瘤
2. 與Primary Hyperaldosteronism 的區別：
• Reninoma: Renin ↑↑↑, Aldosterone normal/↑
• Primary hyperaldosteronism: Renin ↓, Aldosterone ↑↑
3. 病理診斷要點：CD34+, GATA3+, SMA+, Renin+; EM shows rhomboid renin
crystals
4. 治療原則：藥物控制血壓作為橋接→手術切除是根治性治療
5. 預後：大多數是良性，手術切除後可治癒，血壓通常正常化
