frame{REBOOT-CNIC Trial Analysis}
REBOOT-CNIC: Major Findings on $\beta$-Blockers in Non-HF MI Patients
columns
column{0.5\textwidth}
**Study Design:**

- Multinational, Multicenter RCT (109 Hospitals)
- Enrolled 8438 MI Patients with LVEF>40
- Inclusion: STEMI or NSTEMI with Invasive Treatment
- Median Follow-Up: 3.7 Years


**Treatment:**

- $\beta$-Blocker Group vs Control (No $\beta$-Blockers)
- Primarily Bisoprolol (86
- Other Standard Therapies: DAPT 98


column

column{0.5\textwidth}
**Main Results:**

- ****Primary Endpoint****: All-Cause Death + Non-Fatal MI + HF Hospitalization
- ****HR 1.04 (0.89-1.22), p=0.63 (No Significant Difference)****
- No Significant Differences in Individual Components


**Key Findings:**

- **$\beta$-Blockers May Be Harmful in Women**
- Possible Slight Benefit in LVEF 41-49
- Challenges Current Guideline Recommendations


**Safety:**

- Crossover Rate: 7
- Primarily from Control to $\beta$-Blocker Group

frame{TACSI Trial Analysis}
TACSI: Antiplatelet Strategies in ACS Post-CABG Patients
columns
column{0.5\textwidth}
**Study Design:**

- Nordic Multicenter Trial (22 Cardiac Surgery Centers)
- Enrolled 2201 ACS Post-CABG Patients
- Randomized Open-Label Design
- Median Follow-Up: 1 Year


**Treatment:**

- Dual Antiplatelet Therapy: Ticagrelor + Aspirin
- Control: Aspirin Only
- Treatment Duration: 12 Months


column

column{0.5\textwidth}
**Main Results:**

- ****Primary Endpoint****: All-Cause Death + MI + Stroke + Revascularization
- ****HR 1.04 (0.89-1.22), p=0.63 (No Significant Difference)****
- Trend Toward Increased Bleeding Risk


**Clinical Implications:**

- Challenges Guidelines Recommending DAPT in ACS Post-CABG
- Aspirin Alone May Be Sufficient
- Need to Reassess Bleeding vs Ischemic Risk Balance

frame{DIGIT-HF Trial Analysis}
DIGIT-HF: Breakthrough Study on Digitoxin in Heart Failure
columns
column{0.5\textwidth}
**Study Design:**

- Multicenter, Randomized, Double-Blind, Placebo-Controlled Trial
- Enrolled 1212 HFrEF Patients (ITT Population)
- Inclusion: NYHA II with LVEF ≤30
- On Guideline-Directed Therapy for ≥6 Months


**Treatment:**

- Digitoxin Starting Dose 0.07mg QD
- Target Serum Concentration: 8-18 ng/mL
- Median Follow-Up: 36 Months


column

column{0.5\textwidth}
**Main Results:**

- **Primary Endpoint**: All-Cause Death + First HF Hospitalization
- **HR 0.82 (0.69-0.98), p=0.03**
- **Absolute Risk Reduction: 4.6
- **NNT: 22**


**Safety:**

- Serious Adverse Events: 4.7
- Cardiac-Related Adverse Events: 3.4
- Overall Good Safety Profile


**Subgroup Analysis:**

- Consistent Efficacy Across Subgroups
- Includes Patients on ARNI and SGLT2i

frame{VICTOR Trial Analysis}
VICTOR: Large-Scale Vericiguat Trial in Stable Outpatient HFrEF
columns
column{0.5\textwidth}
**Study Design:**

- Phase 3, Double-Blind, Placebo-Controlled Trial
- Enrolled 6105 Outpatient HFrEF Patients
- Inclusion: LVEF ≤40
- NT-proBNP 600-6000 pg/mL
- Median Follow-Up: 18.5 Months


**Treatment:**

- Vericiguat Mean Dose 8.2mg vs Placebo 8.5mg
- On Top of Modern Guideline-Directed Medical Therapy
- SGLT2i Use: 59


column

column{0.5\textwidth}
**Main Results:**

- ****Primary Endpoint****: CV Death or First HF Hospitalization
- ****HR 0.93 (0.83-1.04), p=0.22 (Not Significant)****
- **Secondary Endpoint CV Death**: HR 0.83 (0.71-0.97), p=0.02
- **All-Cause Death**: HR 0.84 (0.74-0.97), p=0.02


**Cause of Death Analysis:**

- Sudden Cardiac Death: HR 0.75 (p=0.04)
- HF-Related Death: HR 0.71 (p=0.02)
- Overall HF Worsening Events: HR 0.90 (p=0.047)


**Safety:**

- Symptomatic Hypotension: 11.3
- Anemia: 7.6
- Overall Acceptable Safety

frame{VICTORION-Difference Trial Analysis}
VICTORION-Difference: Breakthrough Study on Inclisiran for Lipid Lowering
columns
column{0.5\textwidth}
**Study Design:**

- Phase 4, Double-Blind, Placebo-Controlled Trial
- Enrolled 1770 High/Very High CV Risk Patients
- 8 European Countries, 133 Centers
- Mean Patient Age: 63.7 Years


**Treatment:**

- Inclisiran 300mg Subcutaneous (Days 0, 90, 270)
- Combined with Personalized Lipid-Lowering Therapy
- Rosuvastatin Dose Adjusted to Maximum Tolerated


column

column{0.5\textwidth}
**Main Results:**

- **Primary Endpoint**: LDL-C Goal Achievement at Day 90
- **84.9
- **LDL-C Reduction**: -59.45


**Safety Advantages:**

- **Muscle-Related Adverse Events**: 11.9
- **OR 0.57 (0.43-0.74), p<0.0001**
- Mean Rosuvastatin Dose: 13.2mg vs 25.4mg
- Trend Toward Reduced Pain Severity


**Clinical Implications:**

- More Patients Achieve Early LDL-C Goals Without Maximal Statin Doses
- Significantly Fewer Muscle-Related Side Effects
- Offers New Option for Lipid Management
