Hypervirulent Klebsiella pneumoniae Case 2-2026: A 63-Year-Old Man with Pulmonary Nodules, Liver Mass, and Vision Loss 病例2-2026：一位63 歲男性，主訴肺結節、肝臟腫塊與視力喪失 N Engl J Med 2026;394:282-294 DOI: 10.1056/NEJMcpc2402495 January 15, 2026 1 病例呈現(Case Presentation) 1.1 Chief Complaint 主訴 A 63-year-old man was admitted to this hospital with fever, cough, and vision loss in the right eye. 一位63 歲男性因發燒、咳嗽與右眼視力喪失入院。 1.2 History of Present Illness 現病史 The patient had been in his usual state of health until 3 weeks before the current presentation, when vomiting and diarrhea developed, which the patient attributed to having eaten spoiled meat. During the subsequent 2 weeks, vomiting and diarrhea abated, but a new cough pro- ductive of grayish-brown sputum developed, along with intermittent fever and chills. 病人在入院前3 週身體狀況良好，之後出現嘔吐和腹瀉，病人認為是吃了腐壞的肉所致。 接下來2 週內，嘔吐和腹瀉緩解，但出現新的咳嗽伴灰棕色痰液，以及間歇性發燒和寒顫。 Five days before the current presentation, the patient fell in the shower and hit the back of his head (no loss of consciousness). Three days before the current presentation, the cough worsened, and he was brought to the emergency department of another hospital. 入院前5 天，病人在淋浴時跌倒撞到後腦（無意識喪失）。入院前3 天，咳嗽加劇，被送 往另一家醫院急診。 Key Timeline 關鍵時間線： • Week -3: Vomiting and diarrhea (attributed to spoiled meat) 嘔吐腹瀉（歸因於腐 壞肉類） • Week -2 to -1: GI symptoms resolved; new cough with grayish-brown sputum, inter- mittent fever/chills 腸胃症狀緩解；新發咳嗽伴灰棕色痰、間歇性發燒寒顫 • Day -5: Fall in shower, hit head 淋浴時跌倒撞頭 • Day -3: Worsening cough →First ED visit 咳嗽加劇，第一次急診 • Day -1 (Hospital Day 3): Acute vision loss in right eye 右眼急性視力喪失 • Day 0: Transfer to MGH 轉院至MGH
