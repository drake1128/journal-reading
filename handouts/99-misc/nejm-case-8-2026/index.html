Blood/urine cultures and imaging unrevealing. TTE: thickening of mitral-valve leaflets and noncoronary aortic-valve leaflet. Discharged after 10 days with sup- portive care. 入院前6 週：因高燒41.1°C、呼吸困難、低血氧呼吸衰竭、心搏過速、意識改變及 鴉片類藥物注射入院。血液/尿液培養及影像檢查無異常。TTE：二尖瓣葉及非冠 狀動脈主動脈瓣葉增厚。支持治療10 天後出院。 • Several weeks before admission: Progressive cough with yellow sputum, orthop- nea, dyspnea on exertion, exercise intolerance, chills, and lightheadedness. 入院前數週：漸進性黃痰咳嗽、端坐呼吸、運動性呼吸困難、運動不耐、畏寒及頭 暈。 • 2 days before admission: Central, nonexertional, nonradiating chest tightness developed during sleep. 入院前2 天：睡眠中出現中央型、非運動性、無放射性胸部緊迫感。 • Day 0 (Admission): Chest tightness intensified to 10/10; dyspnea at rest; in- sufflated illicit fentanyl; 20 minutes later worsening dyspnea; two brief syncopal episodes; HR 38–68 bpm, SBP as low as 70 mm Hg; RR 43/min; SpO2 93% on room air. 入院當日：胸部緊迫感加劇至10/10 分；靜態呼吸困難；吸入非法芬太尼；20 分 鐘後呼吸困難加重；兩次短暫暈厥發作；心率38–68 bpm，收縮壓低至70 mm Hg。
