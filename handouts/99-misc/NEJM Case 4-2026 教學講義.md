## 7. 討論
7.1 HIV/AIDS in Older Adults
老年人HIV 感染的重要性
流行病學(2021 年美國數據):
• 41% 的HIV 感染者年齡￿55 歲
• 在￿55 歲時診斷HIV 的人中，34% 同時診斷為AIDS
• 意味著診斷時疾病已是晚期
Screening barriers in older adults:
• 就診時需討論的議題太多
• 社會規範使討論性健康較困難
• 可能的embarrassment
• 臨床醫師對老年人性活動的assumptions
Key message: 當存在風險因子時，不論年齡都應進行HIV 篩檢！

7.4 PJP Treatment
PJP 治療原則
First-line treatment:
• High-dose Trimethoprim-Sulfamethoxazole (TMP-SMX)
• Strongly preferred over alternatives due to superior eﬀicacy
• Duration: typically 21 days
• Monitor: potassium levels, kidney function
Adjunctive Glucocorticoid Therapy:
• Indicated for moderate-to-severe PJP:
– PaO2 <70 mmHg on room air, OR
– A-a gradient ￿35 mmHg
• Purpose: attenuate inflammatory response to dying organisms
• Start within 72 hours of antimicrobial therapy
7.5 Cryptococcal Infection 的處理
> **重點提醒**
本病例的Cryptococcal Infection
• BAL culture positive for C. neoformans
• Serum cryptococcal antigen negative (×2)
• Lumbar puncture: 排除CNS involvement
Treatment consideration:
• Negative serum antigen + negative CSF →likely localized pulmonary disease
• Treatment with fluconazole initiated
• Duration: total 6 months
Important: Multiple concurrent opportunistic infections are common when CD4 count is
severely low.
7.6 Antiretroviral Therapy (ART) Timing
ART Initiation Timing
General principle: Early ART leads to:
• Fewer AIDS-defining diseases
• Lower risk of death
• Greater viral suppression
• Better patient retention in care
Exception —Cryptococcal Meningitis:
• Studies from sub-Saharan Africa show increased morbidity/mortality with early
ART
• Guidelines recommend delaying ART in cryptococcal meningitis
This patient:
• CSF analysis ruled out cryptococcal meningitis
• →ART (bictegravir-emtricitabine-tenofovir alafenamide) was started

2. Pneumocystis jirovecii Pneumonia 的臨床特徵
• Subacute course (days to weeks)
• Cough, dyspnea, fever, weight loss
• CD4 count 通常￿200/￿L
• CT: Bilateral ground-glass opacities, central distribution, apical predominance
• Procalcitonin 通常正常（非典型細菌感染）
> **重點提醒**
3. PJP 的診斷
• P. jirovecii 是fungus，不會在routine media 上生長
• DFA (direct fluorescent antibody) 是standard approach
• Induced sputum sensitivity: 67-99% in HIV patients
• BAL sensitivity: 90-99% in HIV patients
• 1,3-￿-D-glucan 是useful adjunct test
> **重點提醒**
4. PJP 治療原則
• First-line: High-dose TMP-SMX
• Duration: 21 days for HIV patients
• Adjunctive steroids: 用於moderate-to-severe PJP (PaO2 <70 mmHg or A-a gradient
￿35 mmHg)
• After treatment: prophylaxis until CD4 >200 for 3 months
> **重點提醒**
5. Multiple Opportunistic Infections
• Severely immunocompromised patients 可有多種concurrent infections
• 本病例：PJP + pulmonary cryptococcosis
• 考慮additional workup when response to treatment is incomplete
> **重點提醒**
6. ART Initiation Timing
• Generally: Early ART improves outcomes
• Exception: Cryptococcal meningitis —delay ART
• 本病例：CSF negative →可以start ART
• U = U: Undetectable = Untransmissible

11.2 答案與解析(Answers and Explanations)
Question 1. Answer: (C)
Explanation: The subacute course (8 weeks), bilateral ground-glass opacities with central
distribution and apical predominance, normal procalcitonin (not elevated in non-bacterial in-
fections), and significant hypoxemia are classic features of Pneumocystis jirovecii pneumonia. The CT pattern is particularly characteristic of PJP. 解析：亞急性病程（8 週）、雙側毛玻璃狀混濁呈中央分佈和上肺優勢、procalcitonin 正常
（非細菌感染時不升高）、以及顯著低血氧，都是肺囊蟲肺炎的經典特徵。CT 影像模式特別
符合PJP。 Question 2. Answer: (C)
Explanation: PJP in HIV patients characteristically presents with bilateral ground-glass
opacities that are central (perihilar) rather than peripheral, with apical predominance and often
sparing of the lung bases. This pattern is present in the majority of HIV-infected patients with
PJP. 解析：HIV 病人的PJP 特徵性表現為雙側毛玻璃狀混濁，呈中央（肺門周圍）而非周邊
分佈，上肺優勢且常常sparing 肺底部。這種模式存在於大多數HIV 感染合併PJP 的病人。 Question 3. Answer: (C)
Explanation: When induced sputum DFA is negative but clinical suspicion remains high
(especially with a strongly positive 1,3-￿-D-glucan), bronchoalveolar lavage should be performed. BAL has superior sensitivity (90-99%) compared to induced sputum (67-99%) for PJP diagnosis
in HIV patients. 解析：當誘導痰液DFA 陰性但臨床懷疑度仍高時（特別是1,3-￿-D-glucan 強陽性），應進
行支氣管肺泡灌洗。BAL 對HIV 病人PJP 診斷的敏感度（90-99%）優於誘導痰液（67-99%）。 Question 4. Answer: (C)
Explanation: Procalcitonin is a serum biomarker that is often elevated in patients with
bacterial infections but not in those with other infectious (viral, fungal) or inflammatory dis-
eases. In this case, normal procalcitonin helped point away from typical bacterial pneumonia. 解析：Procalcitonin 是一種血清生物標記，在細菌感染病人中常升高，但在其他感染性
（病毒、真菌）或發炎性疾病中通常不升高。本病例中，procalcitonin 正常有助於排除典型細
菌性肺炎。 Question 5. Answer: (C)
Explanation: Adjunctive glucocorticoid therapy is indicated for patients with moderate-
to-severe PJP, defined by PaO2 <70 mmHg while breathing ambient air or an alveolar-arterial
gradient ￿35 mmHg. It helps attenuate inflammation from the immune response to dying
organisms. 解析：輔助性類固醇治療適用於中重度PJP 病人，定義為呼吸室內空氣時PaO2 <70
mmHg 或肺泡-動脈梯度￿35 mmHg。它有助於減緩對dying organisms 的免疫反應引起的發
炎。 Question 6. Answer: (C)
Explanation: Cryptococcal antigen testing is most sensitive for disseminated or CNS
disease. It may be negative in cases of isolated pulmonary infection when the fungal burden is
low. This explains the discordance between positive culture and negative antigen test.
解析：隱球菌抗原檢測對播散性或CNS 疾病最敏感。在真菌負荷較低的isolated 肺部感
染病例中可能為陰性。這解釋了培養陽性但抗原檢測陰性的差異。 Question 7. Answer: (B)
Explanation: In 2021, 41% of persons living with HIV in the United States were 55 years of
age or older. Additionally, among those diagnosed when ￿55 years old, 34% received a diagnosis
of AIDS, indicating late diagnosis. HIV screening should be based on risk factors, not age
alone. 解析：2021 年美國41% 的HIV 感染者年齡￿55 歲。此外，在￿55 歲時診斷的人中，34%
同時診斷為AIDS，顯示晚期診斷。HIV 篩檢應基於風險因子，而非僅根據年齡。 Question 8. Answer: (B)
Explanation: High-dose trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole is the first-line treatment for PJP
and is strongly preferred over alternative agents due to superior eﬀicacy. Treatment duration
is typically 21 days for HIV-infected patients. 解析：高劑量trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole 是PJP 的第一線治療，因其療效優越而強
烈優先於其他替代藥物。HIV 感染病人的治療療程通常為21 天。 Question 9. Answer: (B)
Explanation: Early ART initiation is generally recommended as it leads to fewer AIDS-
defining diseases and lower mortality. The exception is cryptococcal meningitis, where delayed
ART is recommended. Since this patient’s CSF was negative for cryptococcal involvement,
ART can be started early. 解析：一般建議早期開始ART，因為這可減少AIDS-defining 疾病和降低死亡率。例外是
隱球菌腦膜炎，建議延遲ART。由於此病人的CSF 無隱球菌侵犯，可以早期開始ART。 Question 10. Answer: (B)
Explanation: While herpes zoster is common in elderly patients, it can also indicate under-
lying immunodeficiency. Combined with travel to HIV-endemic areas (sub-Saharan Africa) and
recent sexual activity, HIV testing is warranted. Risk factor assessment should guide screening
regardless of age. 解析：雖然帶狀疱疹在老年人中常見，但也可能提示underlying 免疫缺陷。結合旅遊至
HIV 流行地區（撒哈拉以南非洲）和近期性活動，應進行HIV 檢測。風險因子評估應指導篩
檢，不論年齡。 12 總結
本病例的關鍵學習要點

年齡都應篩檢
2. Risk Factor Assessment: 旅遊史、性活動史、herpes zoster —三者結合應高度
懷疑HIV
3. PJP 的CT Pattern: Bilateral ground-glass opacities, central distribution, apical
predominance
4. Procalcitonin 的診斷價值: 正常時不支持典型細菌感染，應考慮其他病原體
5. PJP Diagnosis Cascade: Induced sputum DFA →BAL DFA (if negative); 1,3-￿-
D-glucan is useful adjunct
6. Multiple Opportunistic Infections: CD4 極低時可有concurrent infections (PJP
