No OTC decongestants or supplements Family History - Inflammatory bowel disease in twin brothers - No family history of hypertension or heart disease ### 1.4 Physical Examination 身體檢查 Item 項目 Findings 發現 Vital Signs - Answer: (D) Explanation: Reninoma is characterized by markedly elevated plasma renin activity due to autonomous renin secretion by the tumor. In contrast, primary hyperaldosteronism has suppressed/low renin with elevated aldosterone (high aldosterone-to-renin ratio). This is the key distinguishing feature between these two conditions. 解析：腎素瘤的特徵是因腫瘤自主分泌腎素導致血漿腎素活性顯著升高。相比之下，原發 性醛固酮過多症的腎素受抑制/偏低，醛固酮升高（高醛固酮/腎素比值）。這是區分這兩種疾 病的關鍵特徵。 Question 2. Other ECG changes in hypokalemia include ST depression, T wave flattening, and prolonged QT interval. Peaked T waves and widened QRS are seen in hyperkalemia. 解析：U 波是嚴重低血鉀的經典心電圖發現，通常見於V2、V3 和V4 導程。低血鉀的其 他心電圖變化包括ST 段壓低、T 波變平和QT 間期延長。高尖T 波和QRS 波群增寬見於 高血鉀。 Question 3. Answer: (C) Explanation: Studies have linked hyperaldosteronism to idiopathic intracranial hyper- tension. Hyperreninemia in reninoma creates a hyperaldosteronism-like environment. 解析：研究已將醛固酮過多症與原發性顱內高壓聯繫起來。醛固酮刺激脈絡叢上皮細胞的 礦物皮質素受體，導致Na+-K+-ATPase 合成增加，這是CSF 產生的主要驅動因素。腎素瘤 的高腎素血症創造了類似醛固酮過多症的環境。 Question 9.
