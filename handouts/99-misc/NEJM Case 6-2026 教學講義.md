4.1 Pathophysiology of EGPA 嗜酸性肉芽腫性多血管炎的病理生理學
EGPA (formerly Churg–Strauss syndrome) is a mixed allergic and vasculitic disorder. Its
pathogenesis remains unclear, but overproduction of interleukin-5 appears to be a crucial
mediator. EGPA（舊稱Churg–Strauss syndrome）是一種混合過敏性和血管炎性疾病。其病理機轉
尚不明確，但interleukin-5 的過度產生似乎是關鍵介質。 • IL-5 promotes eosinophil differentiation, chemotaxis, activation, and survival
• IL-5 也刺激IgE production，故約70% 的lymphocytic HES 和多數EGPA 患者IgE 升
高
• EGPA is classified as an ANCA-associated vasculitis, along with granulomatosis with
polyangiitis (GPA) and microscopic polyangiitis (MPA)
• 30–40% of EGPA patients have detectable ANCA; most have p-ANCA/anti-MPO
• EGPA is unique: it manifests with hematologic (eosinophilia) and atopic features before
vasculitic features develop

## 7. 學習重點 (Key Takeaways)
> **重點提醒**
1. Systematic Approach to Eosinophilia 嗜酸性球增多的系統性評估
• Eosinophilia (AEC ≥1500/µL) requires systematic evaluation
• First rule out secondary causes: cancer, allergy, adrenal insuﬀiciency, connective-tissue
disorders, parasitic infections
• Then classify into primary HES subtypes using IgE, tryptase, and vitamin B12
• 先排除次級原因，再以IgE、tryptase、vitamin B12 分類至primary HES 亞型
> **重點提醒**
2. Vitamin B12 and Tryptase as Screening Markers for Myeloproliferative HES
• Elevated vitamin B12 (>1000 pg/mL) and tryptase (>11.5 ng/mL) are highly sen-
sitive for myeloproliferative HES
• Normal levels effectively rule out myeloproliferative HES (especially FIP1L1–
PDGFRA fusion)
• This distinction is critical because myeloproliferative HES responds to imatinib
• 正常的B12 和tryptase 可有效排除骨髓增殖型HES；此區分至關重要，因其對
imatinib 高度敏感
> **重點提醒**
3. EGPA: Three-Phase Disease with Distinct Phenotypes
• Classic prodrome: allergic rhinitis, sinusitis, adult-onset asthma (can persist for years)
• Eosinophilic phase: peripheral eosinophilia with organ involvement
• Vasculitic phase: small-vessel vasculitis
• ANCA-positive patients tend toward vasculitic manifestations; ANCA-negative to-
ward allergic/eosinophilic complications
• ANCA 陽性者傾向血管炎表現；ANCA 陰性者傾向過敏/嗜酸性球相關併發症
> **重點提醒**
4. Anti–IL-5 Therapy Revolutionizes EGPA Management
• Mepolizumab and benralizumab reduce relapse risk and enable glucocorticoid tapering
• Can be initiated concurrently with glucocorticoids for earlier tapering
• IL-5 is the central mediator driving both eosinophil expansion and IgE production in
EGPA
• 抗IL-5 治療可降低復發風險並促進類固醇減量
> **重點提醒**
5. CHIP in Elderly Patients 老年人的克隆性造血
• ASXL1 and TET2 mutations were detected but attributed to CHIP, not malignancy
• CHIP prevalence increases with age and does not necessarily indicate hematologic
cancer
• Careful clinical correlation is essential to avoid overdiagnosis
• CHIP 盛行率隨年齡增加，需仔細臨床判讀以避免過度診斷

### 8.2 答案與解析 (Answers and Explanations)
Question 1. Answer: (C)
Explanation: The first step in evaluating a patient with eosinophilia is to rule out all
secondary (nonhematologic) causes, including cancer, allergy, adrenal insuﬀiciency, connective-
tissue disorders, and parasitic infections. Only after secondary causes are excluded should
primary hypereosinophilic syndrome subtypes be considered. 解析：評估嗜酸性球增多的第一步是排除所有次級（非血液學）原因，包括惡性腫瘤、過
敏、腎上腺功能不足、結締組織疾病和寄生蟲感染。只有在排除次級原因後，才考慮原發性
HES 亞型。 Question 2. Answer: (B)
Explanation: Elevated blood levels of vitamin B12 (>1000 pg/mL) and tryptase (>11.5
ng/mL) are highly sensitive for myeloproliferative hypereosinophilic syndrome, particularly in
patients with FIP1L1–PDGFRA or KIT fusions. Tryptase elevation occurs when the fusion
drives expansion of both mast-cell and eosinophil lineages from a common clonal precursor. 解析：Vitamin B12 升高（>1000 pg/mL）和tryptase 升高（>11.5 ng/mL）對骨髓增殖
型HES 高度敏感。Tryptase 升高是因為FIP1L1–PDGFRA fusion 存在於同時驅動肥大細胞
和嗜酸性球增殖的共同克隆前驅細胞中。 Question 3. Answer: (D)
Explanation: Normal vitamin B12 and tryptase levels effectively rule out myeloprolif-
erative HES. Elevated IgE suggests IL-5–driven eosinophilia, which is characteristic of both
lymphocytic HES (where aberrant T cells produce IL-5) and overlap HES including EGPA. Approximately 70% of lymphocytic HES patients have elevated IgE, as IL-5 stimulates both
eosinophil and IgE production. 解析：正常的vitamin B12 和tryptase 可排除骨髓增殖型HES。IgE 升高提示IL-5 驅動
的嗜酸性球增多，這是lymphocytic HES 和overlap HES（包括EGPA）的特徵。IL-5 同時
刺激嗜酸性球和IgE 的產生。 Question 4. Answer: (D)
Explanation: While both EGPA and lymphocytic HES can cause eosinophilia with ele-
vated IgE and respond to anti–IL-5 therapy, EGPA is characterized by a prodrome of allergic
rhinitis, chronic sinusitis, and adult-onset asthma. Lymphocytic HES is strongly associ-
ated with cutaneous manifestations (pruritus, urticaria, eczema) in approximately 80% of cases. This patient lacked cutaneous findings but had prominent upper-airway symptoms, favoring
EGPA. 解析：EGPA 的特徵是過敏性鼻炎、慢性鼻竇炎和成人發病型氣喘的前驅期。淋巴球型
HES 約80% 有皮膚表現。此病人無皮膚發現但有明顯上呼吸道症狀，支持EGPA。 Question 5. Answer: (C)
Explanation: Approximately 50% of patients with EGPA are ANCA-negative. Among
ANCA-positive patients, 30–40% have p-ANCA (anti-MPO) and only about 5% have c-ANCA
(anti-PR3). ANCA-positive patients tend to have more vasculitic manifestations, while ANCA-
negative patients are more likely to have allergy-related or eosinophil-related complications. 解析：約50% 的EGPA 病人ANCA 陰性。ANCA 陽性者中，30–40% 為p-ANCA（anti-
MPO），僅約5% 為c-ANCA（anti-PR3）。ANCA 陽性者傾向血管炎表現，ANCA 陰性者傾
向過敏/嗜酸性球相關併發症。

Explanation: The classic three-phase course of EGPA includes: (1) allergic prodrome

Explanation: The goals of induction therapy for EGPA are to reduce symptoms, prevent

Explanation: Benralizumab targets the IL-5 receptor α on eosinophils and depletes them
