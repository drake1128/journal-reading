### 4.1 Pathophysiology of Hypervirulent K. pneumoniae 高毒力克雷伯

氏肺炎桿菌的病理生理學
Classical vs. Hypervirulent K. pneumoniae:
經典型與高毒力型克雷伯氏肺炎桿菌比較：


- Classical K. pneumoniae: Common cause of nosocomial infections (pneumonia, UTI,
bacteremia); often in immunocompromised hosts; associated with antibiotic resistance
(ESBL, carbapenemases)
- Hypervirulent K. pneumoniae (hvKp): Causes community-acquired invasive infec-
tions in otherwise healthy individuals; characterized by primary liver abscess with
metastatic spread
- 經典型：醫療照護相關感染的常見病因；常見於免疫功能低下宿主；與抗生素抗藥性相
關
- 高毒力型：在健康個體中引起社區型侵入性感染；以原發性肝膿瘍伴轉移性擴散為特徵
Virulence Factors 毒力因子：
1. Hypermucoviscous capsule: K1 or K2 serotypes; detected by positive string test (>5
mm)
2. Virulence plasmids: Carrying rmpA (regulator of mucoid phenotype) and iuc (aer-
obactin siderophore)
3. Enhanced iron acquisition: Aerobactin and other siderophores

## 7. 學習重點(Key Takeaways)


> **重點提醒**
> 1. Recognition of Hypervirulent K. pneumoniae Syndrome 辨識高毒力克雷伯氏
> 肺炎桿菌症候群
> • Classic triad: Pyogenic liver abscess + metastatic infection + (often) endophthalmitis
> • Occurs in otherwise healthy individuals (unlike classical Kp nosocomial infec-
> tions)
> • First described in Taiwan (1980s); endemic in Southeast Asia
> • 經典三聯徵：化膿性肝膿瘍+ 轉移性感染+（常有）眼內炎
> • 發生於健康個體（不同於經典型醫療照護相關感染）


> **重點提醒**
> 2. Liver Abscess Features Suggestive of K. pneumoniae 提示克雷伯氏肺炎桿菌的
> 肝膿瘍特徵
> • Single lesion (more common than with other bacteria)
> • Solid or containing necrotic debris
> • Associated hepatic vein thrombophlebitis
> • Metastatic infection (lungs, brain, eyes)
> • No underlying biliary tract disease


> **重點提醒**
> 3. Endogenous Endophthalmitis Due to K. pneumoniae 克雷伯氏肺炎桿菌導致
> 的內源性眼內炎
> • Occurs in 7% of patients with Kp liver abscess
> • Dense fibrin in anterior chamber (characteristic finding)
> • Rapid progression; may lead to panophthalmitis
> • Visual prognosis: >70% permanent disability
> • May require enucleation if uncontrolled


> **重點提醒**
> 4. Diagnostic Approach 診斷方法
> • String test: Positive if viscous string >5 mm (hypermucoviscosity)
> • Limitation: Not sensitive or specific; genomic testing preferred
> • Blood cultures may be negative if antibiotics given
> • Consider vitreous/aqueous culture or PCR for eye involvement
> • 黏絲試驗：黏絲>5mm 為陽性（高黏液黏性）
> • 限制：敏感度和特異度不高；基因檢測為首選


> **重點提醒**
> 5. Treatment Principles 治療原則
> • hvKp typically susceptible to common antibiotics (unlike MDR classical strains)
> • Prolonged antibiotic therapy required (weeks to months)
> • Drainage of liver abscess when feasible; medical management if not
> • Intravitreal antibiotics for endophthalmitis
> • Emerging concern: Carbapenem-resistant hvKp (>50% mortality)


> **重點提醒**
> 6. Association with Colorectal Cancer 與大腸直腸癌的關聯
> • Patients with pyogenic liver abscess have >2× risk of CRC
> • Risk even higher if caused by K. pneumoniae
> • Anemia + weight loss should prompt colonoscopy
> • 化膿性肝膿瘍病人罹患大腸直腸癌風險增加>2 倍
> • 若由克雷伯氏肺炎桿菌引起，風險更高
