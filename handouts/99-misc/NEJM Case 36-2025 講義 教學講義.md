4.3 Obscure Gastrointestinal Bleeding 的定義
Obscure GI Bleeding
定義: Persistent or recurrent bleeding with no source identified on upper and lower
endoscopic evaluation. 本病例情況:
• 2 次EGD (含enteroscopy 至proximal jejunum)
• 1 次colonoscopy
• 1 次video-capsule endoscopy
• 均未發現出血來源
可能原因:
1. 先前檢查遺漏出血來源(如Dieulafoy’s lesion 等subtle mucosal abnormality)
2. 出血來自尚未檢查的區域—Roux limb!

8.3 鑑別診斷討論的寫作模式
Differential Diagnosis Discussion Framework
1. 建立診斷框架:
“I will approach this patient’s case by attempting to establish the anatomical
location of the source of bleeding and the mechanistic cause of her bleeding.”
2. 分析臨床線索:
“The color of the stool can provide insight into the general location of gas-
trointestinal bleeding. This patient initially presented with hematochezia... The subsequent occurrence of rectal bleeding that was described as melena is
consistent with an upper gastrointestinal source.”
3. 定義臨床情境:
“The patient’s presentation can be classified as obscure gastrointestinal bleed-
ing —persistent or recurrent bleeding with no source identified on upper and
lower endoscopic evaluation.”
4. 提出鑑別診斷:
“Although cirrhosis accounts for the majority of cases of portal hypertensive
bleeding, the possibility of noncirrhotic portal hypertension is a consideration
in this patient.”
5. 排除其他診斷:
“A cancer in the hilum, such as cholangiocarcinoma, should be considered... but this possibility seems unlikely on the basis of the patient’s normal bilirubin
and alkaline phosphatase levels, the absence of weight loss or other constitu-
tional symptoms, and the absence of a hilar mass on MRCP.”
6. 得出最可能診斷:
“The most likely cause of this patient’s bleeding is a jejunal varix.”

9.1 考題詳解
1. 正確答案: c) Upper gastrointestinal tract
詳解: 病人同時出現hematochezia(血便) 和melena(黑便), 其中melena 提示出血經
過上消化道的消化過程。當上消化道大量出血時, 可能因為腸道通過時間快而呈現
hematochezia。兩種糞便型態同時出現, 最一致的解釋是upper GI source。原文明確
指出:”Since this patient had passed both stool containing red blood and melena, the
location of the bleeding is most likely in the upper gastrointestinal tract.”
2. 正確答案: b) Splenic sequestration due to portal hypertension
詳解: Iron-deficiency anemia 通常會導致reactive thrombocytosis。然而此病人呈現
thrombocytopenia, 這是一個異常發現。在portal hypertension 的情況下,thrombocytopenia
可因splenic sequestration of platelets 而發生, 且在chronic liver disease 病人中也可能
因decreased thrombopoietin production 所致。這個發現是提示underlying portal hy-
pertension 的重要線索。 3. 正確答案: b) 上下消化道內視鏡檢查均未發現出血來源
詳解: Obscure gastrointestinal bleeding 的定義是persistent or recurrent bleeding with no
source identified on upper and lower endoscopic evaluation。此病人接受了2 次EGD(含
enteroscopy)、1 次colonoscopy 及1 次video-capsule endoscopy, 均未發現出血來源, 符
合obscure GI bleeding 的定義。 4. 正確答案: c) Postsurgical fibrosis from complicated cholecystectomy
詳解: 此病人20 年前接受cholecystectomy 時併發bile duct laceration, 需要open repair
及hepaticojejunostomy。Portal vein stenosis 最可能是由於手術後的inflammation、
adhesions 及fibrotic remodeling 所導致。影像上的caudate lobe atrophy 及perihilar
region 的abnormal signal intensity and delayed enhancement 都支持這個病因。病人沒
有chronic liver disease 的危險因子或表現, 排除了cirrhotic portal hypertension。 5. 正確答案: c) Portosystemic collaterals near hepaticojejunostomy site on imag-
ing
詳解: CT angiography 顯示portosystemic collaterals in the hepatic hilum, 位於hep-
aticojejunostomy site 附近。這個位置的側枝循環與病人的手術解剖結構一致, 提示
jejunal varices 的存在。Video-capsule endoscopy 是negative 的, 而且傳統內視鏡無法
到達Roux limb。Esophageal varices 在本病例中並未被提及。 6. 正確答案: c) Hemodynamically significant stenosis
詳解: 正常portal vein pressure gradient 應小於5 mmHg。此病人測得11 mmHg 的壓
力梯度, 代表hemodynamically significant stenosis。這個發現確認了portal vein stenosis
是造成portal hypertension 及jejunal varices 的原因。治療後壓力梯度降至2 mmHg,
證實了治療的有效性。 7. 正確答案: c) TIPS (transjugular intrahepatic portosystemic shunt)
詳解: 此病人接受的介入治療包括:(1) Portal vein stenting with 12-mm × 60-mm self-
expanding bare-metal stent,(2) Jejunal variceal embolization with N-butyl-2-cyanoacrylate
(NBCA),(3) Tract embolization with coils and absorbable gelatin pledgets。TIPS 並非此
次治療的一部分, 因為病因是portal vein stenosis 而非intrahepatic portal hypertension。

enhancement, which indicated decreased filling of the varices near the hepaticojejunos-

詳解: Ectopic varices 是指發生在非典型位置(食道、胃底以外) 的靜脈曲張。它們可

可能導致嚴重出血, 需要積極治療。 10 總結
本病例的關鍵學習要點
1. Obscure GI bleeding 需考慮altered anatomy: 有外科手術史(尤其是biliary
or GI surgery) 的病人, 可能有標準內視鏡無法到達的區域
2. Thrombocytopenia in iron-deficiency anemia 是異常的: 應提示underlying
portal hypertension 的可能性
3. Noncirrhotic portal hypertension: 可由postsurgical fibrosis 導致, 即使發生在
手術後多年
4. Ectopic varices: 可發生在任何portal hypertensive 病人的surgical anastomosis
site
5. Portal venography with pressure measurement: 是確認hemodynamically
significant stenosis 的關鍵診斷工具
6. Combined approach: Portal vein stenting + variceal embolization 可有效治療此
類病變
