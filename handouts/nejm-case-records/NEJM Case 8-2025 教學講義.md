importantbox
**Why was the initial urine ketone test negative? 為何初始尿酮測試為陰性？**

The urine dipstick test for ketones uses sodium nitroprusside, which reacts with **acetoacetate** but does NOT react with **$\beta$-hydroxybutyrate**. In DKA, the ratio of $\beta$-hydroxybutyrate to acetoacetate shifts from the normal 3:1 to as high as 10:1 due to the reduced mitochondrial redox state. Therefore, blood $\beta$-hydroxybutyrate measurement is the more accurate diagnostic test.

尿酮試紙使用亞硝基鐵氰化鈉，與乙醯乙酸反應，但不會與$\beta$-羥丁酸反應。在DKA中，由於線粒體氧化還原狀態改變，$\beta$-羥丁酸與乙醯乙酸的比例從正常的3:1升至高達10:1。因此，血清$\beta$-羥丁酸測量是更準確的診斷方法。

## 討論 (Discussion)


### Pathophysiology of SGLT2 Inhibitor-Induced Euglycemic DKA


SGLT2 inhibitors (such as dapagliflozin, empagliflozin, canagliflozin) work by inhibiting sodium-glucose cotransporter 2 in the proximal tubules of the kidneys, reducing glucose reabsorption and causing glycosuria.

SGLT2抑制劑（如dapagliflozin、empagliflozin、canagliflozin）透過抑制腎臟近端小管的鈉-葡萄糖共同轉運蛋白2，減少葡萄糖再吸收並引起糖尿。

**Mechanism of Euglycemic DKA 血糖正常型DKA的機轉**:


1. **Reduced blood glucose** $\rightarrow$ Decreased insulin secretion 血糖降低 $\rightarrow$ 胰島素分泌減少
2. **Direct effect on pancreatic alpha cells** $\rightarrow$ Increased glucagon secretion 直接作用於胰臟$\alpha$細胞 $\rightarrow$ 升糖素分泌增加
3. **Volume depletion** from glucosuria-induced diuresis 糖尿引起利尿導致血容量減少
4. **Low insulin + High glucagon** $\rightarrow$ Promotes hepatic $\beta$-oxidation of fatty acids $\rightarrow$ Ketogenesis 低胰島素+高升糖素 $\rightarrow$ 促進肝臟脂肪酸$\beta$-氧化 $\rightarrow$ 生酮作用
5. **Accumulation of ketone bodies** (acetoacetate, $\beta$-hydroxybutyrate) $\rightarrow$ Metabolic acidosis 酮體累積 $\rightarrow$ 代謝性酸中毒
6. Blood glucose remains **near-normal** because glucose is continuously excreted in urine 血糖維持接近正常，因為葡萄糖持續從尿液排出


**Precipitating Factors in This Case 本案例的誘發因素**:

- Perioperative fasting state (scheduled for bladder fulguration) 手術前禁食狀態
- Possible urinary tract infection 可能的泌尿道感染
- Increased diuretic doses (volume depletion) 增加利尿劑劑量（血容量減少）
- Acute-on-chronic kidney injury (reduced clearance of ketones) 急性疊加慢性腎損傷（酮體清除率降低）


SGLT2 inhibitors have a long duration of action. Dapagliflozin has a half-life of 12.9 hours, and its pharmacologic effects can persist for 2-3 days (even up to 9 days) after discontinuation.

SGLT2抑制劑作用持續時間長。Dapagliflozin半衰期為12.9小時，其藥理作用可在停藥後持續2-3天（甚至長達9天）。


### Why Lactate Was Normal Despite Shock and Metformin Use


Metformin blocks the mitochondrial electron transport chain (Complex I), which normally directs glucose toward lactate production. However, in patients taking both dapagliflozin and metformin with euglycemic DKA:

Metformin阻斷線粒體電子傳遞鏈（複合物I），正常情況下會導致葡萄糖轉向乳酸生成。然而，在同時服用dapagliflozin和metformin且有血糖正常型DKA的病人中：


- Low intracellular glucose (due to SGLT2 inhibitor) impedes lactate production 細胞內葡萄糖低（因SGLT2抑制劑）阻礙乳酸生成
- Metabolism shifts toward Krebs cycle intermediates and ketone production instead 代謝轉向克雷布斯循環中間產物和酮體生成
- This may result in a lower lactate level than would otherwise be expected 這可能導致乳酸濃度低於通常預期的水平

### Management Principles 治療原則


**1. Fluid Management 液體管理**

- Initial fluid therapy aims to expand intravascular, interstitial, and intracellular volume 初始液體治療旨在擴張血管內、間質及細胞內容積
- In patients with heart failure, balance crystalloid needs with risk of pulmonary edema exacerbation 在心衰竭病人，需平衡晶體液需求與加重肺水腫的風險
- Goal: Correct estimated deficits within 24-48 hours 目標：在24-48小時內糾正估計的液體缺失


**2. Insulin and Glucose Management 胰島素及血糖管理**

- In euglycemic DKA, start dextrose infusion simultaneously with insulin 在血糖正常型DKA，同時開始葡萄糖及胰島素輸注
- Target blood glucose: 150-200 mg/dL 目標血糖：150-200 mg/dL
- Continue insulin until ketoacidosis resolves (anion gap normalizes, pH normalizes) 持續胰島素直到酮酸中毒緩解


**3. Potassium Management 鉀離子管理**

- Nearly all DKA patients have total body potassium deficit despite normal or elevated serum levels 幾乎所有DKA病人儘管血清鉀正常或升高，但全身鉀離子缺乏
- Initiate replacement when K+ falls below upper normal range 當K+降至正常上限以下時開始補充
- If hypokalemic on presentation, delay insulin until K+ normalizes 若入院時低血鉀，延遲胰島素直到K+正常化


**4. Medications to Discontinue 需停用的藥物**

- **SGLT2 inhibitor (dapagliflozin)** - Discontinue indefinitely 永久停用
- **Metformin** - Discontinue indefinitely (risk of lactic acidosis in renal impairment) 永久停用（腎功能不全時有乳酸酸中毒風險）


### Clinical Outcome 臨床結果


Within 9 hours of starting intravenous insulin therapy:

開始靜脈胰島素治療後9小時內：

- Anion gap decreased by more than half 陰離子間隙下降超過一半
- Arterial pH and bicarbonate levels normalized 動脈血pH及碳酸氫根濃度正常化
- Renal function returned to baseline 腎功能恢復至基礎值
- Metformin and SGLT2 inhibitor discontinued indefinitely; repaglinide started 永久停用metformin及SGLT2抑制劑；開始使用repaglinide
- Eight weeks later, uneventful transurethral resection of bladder tumor 八週後順利完成經尿道膀胱腫瘤切除術


## 學習重點 (Key Takeaways)


**Clinical Pearl 1**: Euglycemic DKA is a diagnostic challenge because the absence of marked hyperglycemia can delay recognition. Always consider this diagnosis in patients on SGLT2 inhibitors presenting with metabolic acidosis.

**臨床要點1**：血糖正常型DKA是診斷上的挑戰，因為沒有明顯高血糖可能延遲診斷。在使用SGLT2抑制劑且出現代謝性酸中毒的病人，務必考慮此診斷。

**Clinical Pearl 2**: Urine dipstick testing for ketones can yield false negative results because it detects acetoacetate, not $\beta$-hydroxybutyrate. In DKA, $\beta$-hydroxybutyrate predominates (ratio up to 10:1). Always measure serum $\beta$-hydroxybutyrate for accurate diagnosis.

**臨床要點2**：尿液試紙酮體檢測可能假陰性，因為它偵測乙醯乙酸而非$\beta$-羥丁酸。在DKA中，$\beta$-羥丁酸占主導（比例可達10:1）。務必測量血清$\beta$-羥丁酸以準確診斷。

**Clinical Pearl 3**: GOLD MARK is a useful mnemonic for high anion-gap metabolic acidosis: **G**lycols, **O**xoproline, **L**-lactate, **D**-lactate, **M**ethanol, **A**spirin, **R**enal failure, **K**etoacidosis.

**臨床要點3**：GOLD MARK是高陰離子間隙代謝性酸中毒的實用記憶口訣。

**Clinical Pearl 4**: 3+ glycosuria with near-normal blood glucose is a hallmark finding of SGLT2 inhibitor use and should raise suspicion for euglycemic DKA in the appropriate clinical context.

**臨床要點4**：血糖接近正常但尿糖3+是SGLT2抑制劑使用的特徵性發現，在適當的臨床情境下應提高對血糖正常型DKA的懷疑。

**Clinical Pearl 5**: SGLT2 inhibitors have prolonged pharmacologic effects (2-9 days after discontinuation). Perioperative fasting, infection, and volume depletion are common precipitants of euglycemic DKA in these patients.

**臨床要點5**：SGLT2抑制劑藥理效應持續時間長（停藥後2-9天）。手術前禁食、感染和血容量減少是這些病人血糖正常型DKA的常見誘因。

importantbox
**Perioperative Recommendations for SGLT2 Inhibitors SGLT2抑制劑圍手術期建議**:

Current guidelines recommend discontinuing SGLT2 inhibitors **3-4 days before elective surgery** to reduce the risk of perioperative euglycemic DKA. Monitor for ketoacidosis if surgery cannot be delayed.

目前指引建議在**擇期手術前3-4天停用**SGLT2抑制劑，以降低圍手術期血糖正常型DKA的風險。若手術無法延遲，需監測酮酸中毒。

### 答案與解析 (Answers and Explanations)


**Question 1. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: Elevated serum $\beta$-hydroxybutyrate is the most accurate diagnostic test for diabetic ketoacidosis. The blood level directly measures the predominant ketone body in DKA. Urine dipstick testing (B) can be falsely negative because it detects acetoacetate, not $\beta$-hydroxybutyrate.

**解析**：血清$\beta$-羥丁酸升高是糖尿病酮酸中毒最準確的診斷測試。血液濃度直接測量DKA中主要的酮體。尿液試紙測試（B）可能假陰性，因為它偵測乙醯乙酸而非$\beta$-羥丁酸。


**Question 2. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: The urine dipstick test uses sodium nitroprusside, which reacts with acetoacetate but does NOT react with $\beta$-hydroxybutyrate. In DKA, the ratio of $\beta$-hydroxybutyrate to acetoacetate shifts from 3:1 to as high as 10:1, making urine testing unreliable.

**解析**：尿液試紙使用亞硝基鐵氰化鈉，與乙醯乙酸反應但不與$\beta$-羥丁酸反應。在DKA中，$\beta$-羥丁酸與乙醯乙酸的比例從3:1升至高達10:1，使尿液測試不可靠。


**Question 3. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: Current guidelines recommend discontinuing SGLT2 inhibitors 3-4 days before elective surgery to reduce the risk of perioperative euglycemic DKA. This accounts for the prolonged pharmacologic effects of these medications.

**解析**：目前指引建議在擇期手術前3-4天停用SGLT2抑制劑，以降低圍手術期血糖正常型DKA的風險。


**Question 4. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: In the GOLD MARK mnemonic, "O" stands for 5-Oxoproline (pyroglutamic acid). This can accumulate in patients with chronic acetaminophen use, particularly those with renal insufficiency and malnutrition.

**解析**：在GOLD MARK記憶口訣中，「O」代表5-Oxoproline（焦谷胺酸）。這可能在長期使用acetaminophen的病人中累積。


**Question 5. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: An elevated osmolar gap ($>$20 mOsm/kg) in the setting of high anion-gap metabolic acidosis strongly suggests toxic alcohol ingestion (methanol or ethylene glycol). These substances and their metabolites contribute to the osmolar gap.

**解析**：在高陰離子間隙代謝性酸中毒情況下，滲透壓間隙升高（$>$20 mOsm/kg）強烈提示毒性醇類攝入（甲醇或乙二醇）。


**Question 6. Answer: (B)**

**Explanation**: SGLT2 inhibitors reduce glucose reabsorption in the proximal tubules, causing glucosuria. This lowers blood glucose, which decreases insulin secretion and increases glucagon secretion. The low insulin/high glucagon state promotes hepatic $\beta$-oxidation and ketogenesis.

**解析**：SGLT2抑制劑減少近端小管的葡萄糖再吸收，導致糖尿。這降低血糖，從而減少胰島素分泌並增加升糖素分泌。低胰島素/高升糖素狀態促進肝臟$\beta$-氧化和生酮作用。


**Question 7. Answer: (B)**

**Explanation**: In euglycemic DKA, blood glucose is already near-normal. Therefore, dextrose infusion must be started simultaneously with insulin to prevent hypoglycemia while allowing adequate insulin dosing to suppress ketogenesis.

**解析**：在血糖正常型DKA中，血糖已經接近正常。因此，必須同時開始葡萄糖輸注和胰島素，以防止低血糖同時允許足夠的胰島素劑量抑制生酮作用。


**Question 8. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: Septic shock with tissue hypoperfusion causes type A lactic acidosis due to anaerobic metabolism. A normal lactate level makes this diagnosis very unlikely.

**解析**：組織低灌注的敗血性休克因無氧代謝導致A型乳酸酸中毒。正常乳酸濃度使這個診斷非常不可能。


**Question 9. Answer: (B)**

**Explanation**: Metformin normally blocks the mitochondrial electron transport chain, directing glucose toward lactate production. However, in patients also taking SGLT2 inhibitors, the low intracellular glucose level impedes lactate production. Instead, metabolism shifts toward ketone production.

**解析**：Metformin通常阻斷線粒體電子傳遞鏈，導致葡萄糖轉向乳酸生成。然而，在同時服用SGLT2抑制劑的病人中，細胞內葡萄糖濃度低阻礙乳酸生成。代謝反而轉向酮體生成。

**Explanation**: After an episode of SGLT2 inhibitor-induced euglycemic DKA, the medication should be discontinued indefinitely due to the risk of recurrence. An alternative diabetes medication should be prescribed.

**解析**：在發生SGLT2抑制劑誘發的血糖正常型DKA後，該藥物應永久停用以避免復發風險。應處方替代的糖尿病藥物。
