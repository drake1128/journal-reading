**Clinical Pearl: $\alpha$-Defensin與人工關節周圍感染**

$\alpha$-Defensin是活化的neutrophils和macrophages產生的antimicrobial peptide。**Synovial fluid $\alpha$-defensin陽性對PJI (periprosthetic joint infection，人工關節周圍感染) 的診斷具有高度特異性**，即使在培養陰性的情況下仍有診斷價值。但在adverse local tissue reaction to metal、crystalline arthropathy或inflammatory arthritis時可能出現偽陽性。

**Clinical Pearl 臨床要點：培養陰性的PJI——想到分枝桿菌**

### BCG: From Vaccine to Cancer Therapy to Infection


- **BCG (bacille Calmette--Gu\'{e**rin)}: Attenuated strain of *Mycobacterium bovis*, initially developed as a vaccine against *M. tuberculosis*
- Recognized as an immunotherapeutic agent against cancer in the 1970s; now standard care for **bladder cancer without muscle invasion**
- **Mechanism**: Intravesical BCG induces active mycobacterial replication within host tissues $\rightarrow$ local immune response against cancer
- **Infection after BCG**: Occurs in up to 4     itemize
- Local genitourinary infection
- Disseminated disease（播散性疾病）
- Rare BCG sepsis（罕見BCG敗血症）


    \item Similar to *M. tuberculosis*, **BCG may establish latency**（可建立潛伏感染）
    \item On reactivation: subacute onset, can include **inflammatory masses**（再活化時呈亞急性，可形成發炎性腫塊）
    \item Osteoarticular complications: PJI typically occurs **months to years** after BCG administration
itemize

### Treatment Course and Outcome 治療經過及結果


- Treatment with rifampin, isoniazid, ethambutol, and pyridoxine started
- 3 weeks later: generalized rash $\rightarrow$ all antimycobacterial therapy discontinued; rash resolved
- Re-introduction one agent per week: isoniazid tolerated; ethambutol caused pruritic urticarial rash $\rightarrow$ permanently discontinued
- Patient expressed substantial distress about treatment toxicity and monitoring burden
- Once isolate confirmed as *M. bovis* BCG: offered isoniazid + rifampin alone $\rightarrow$ **patient declined further treatment**
- **6 months after surgery**: Healed well, no evidence of infection relapse or pain; continues to live independently
- 6個月後：傷口癒合良好，無感染復發或疼痛跡象；持續獨立生活


## 學習重點 (Key Takeaways)


**1. PJI Diagnosis: Culture-Negative Does Not Mean Infection-Free**

- Synovial fluid culture sensitivity for PJI is only 65--78
- Negative cultures may be due to fastidious organisms (e.g., mycobacteria), biofilm, prior antibiotics, or organism location in tissue rather than fluid
- $\alpha$-Defensin and synovial fluid cell count/differential are valuable adjuncts when cultures are negative
- 培養陰性不等於無感染；$\alpha$-defensin和滑液細胞分析是培養陰性時的重要輔助診斷工具


**2. BCG Therapy Can Cause Late-Onset Osteoarticular Infection**

- Intravesical BCG for bladder cancer can lead to active mycobacterial replication and dissemination
- Similar to *M. tuberculosis*, BCG can establish latency and reactivate months to years later
- Osteoarticular BCG infection, including PJI, is rare but well-documented
- A history of BCG therapy + sepsis should raise suspicion for mycobacterial PJI, especially with indolent presentation
- BCG治療後的敗血症病史應提高對分枝桿菌PJI的懷疑


**3. *M. bovis** Has Intrinsic Pyrazinamide Resistance*

- *M. bovis* (including BCG) carries a characteristic *pncA* gene mutation (e.g., H57D)
- This confers intrinsic pyrazinamide resistance --- pyrazinamide must NOT be included in the regimen
- Species-level identification within the *M. tuberculosis* complex is essential for appropriate treatment
- *M. bovis*具有固有pyrazinamide抗性，治療方案不可包含pyrazinamide


**4. One-Stage vs. Two-Stage Revision for PJI**

- Two-stage revision is the traditional standard but requires prolonged immobilization and repeat surgery
- One-stage revision may be appropriate for: elderly/frail patients, indolent infections, patients with poor soft-tissue healing potential
- Decision should balance infection control with patient-centered factors (functional status, comorbidities, quality of life)
- 一期翻修適用於高齡體弱、亞急性感染、軟組織癒合能力差的病人


**5. Patient-Centered Care in Complex Infectious Disease Management**

- This patient ultimately declined antimycobacterial therapy due to side effects and monitoring burden
- Despite declining treatment, he remained infection-free at 6 months post-surgery
- Extensive risk--benefit counseling and shared decision-making are essential
- Antimicrobial stewardship must balance infection control with patient autonomy and quality of life
- 抗微生物治療需平衡感染控制與病人自主權及生活品質

### 答案與解析 (Answers and Explanations)


**Question 1. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: The combination of elevated synovial fluid nucleated-cell count ($\geq$3,000/\textmu L), neutrophil predominance ($\geq$75

**解析**：滑液核細胞數升高（$\geq$3,000/\textmu L）、嗜中性球為主（第二次穿刺79


**Question 2. Answer: (B)**

**Explanation**: Negative cultures in PJI can be explained by fastidious organisms (e.g., mycobacteria requiring special media), biofilm organisms on the implant surface with reduced replication rates, organisms residing in periprosthetic tissue rather than shedding into fluid, or prior antibiotic exposure. Negative cultures do NOT rule out infection.

**解析**：培養陰性可因苛養性微生物（如需特殊培養基的分枝桿菌）、植入物表面biofilm、微生物存在於組織而非滑液中、或先前抗生素暴露。培養陰性不能排除感染。


**Question 3. Answer: (B)**

**Explanation**: A meta-analysis by the international consensus working group on PJI definition found that a synovial fluid leukocyte count $\geq$3,000/\textmu L or neutrophil percentage $\geq$75

**解析**：國際共識工作小組的meta-analysis發現滑液白血球$\geq$3,000/\textmu L或嗜中性球比例$\geq$75


**Question 4. Answer: (B)**

**Explanation**: BCG (bacille Calmette--Gu\'{e}rin) is an attenuated strain of *Mycobacterium bovis*, a bovine tuberculosis species closely related to *M. tuberculosis*. It was developed as a vaccine against TB and is now also used as intravesical immunotherapy for non-muscle-invasive bladder cancer.

**解析**：BCG是*Mycobacterium bovis*（牛分枝桿菌）的減毒株，與*M. tuberculosis*密切相關。最初開發為TB疫苗，現也用於非肌肉侵犯性膀胱癌的膀胱內免疫治療。


**Question 5. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: *M. bovis* (including the BCG strain) has intrinsic pyrazinamide resistance due to a characteristic mutation in the *pncA* gene (e.g., H57D). This is a key distinguishing feature from *M. tuberculosis* and has direct treatment implications --- pyrazinamide must be excluded from the regimen.

**解析**：*M. bovis*因*pncA*基因特徵性突變（如H57D）而具有固有pyrazinamide抗性。這是與*M. tuberculosis*的關鍵區別，直接影響治療方案。


**Question 6. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: Direct inoculation is unlikely given the 20-year interval since arthroplasty. Contiguous spread is unlikely because the ulcer was at the distal leg, distant from the knee. The most likely mechanism is hematogenous seeding from BCG-related sepsis that occurred after the 10th cycle of intravesical BCG therapy, with subsequent latency and reactivation in the periprosthetic joint.

**解析**：距TKA已20年排除直接接種；潰瘍在小腿遠端排除鄰近擴散。最可能是第10次BCG治療後的敗血症導致血行性播種，隨後在人工關節周圍潛伏並再活化。


**Question 7. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: A nucleic acid test designed for species discrimination was used, which amplifies four genomic regions whose presence varies among species within the *M. tuberculosis* complex. A pan-complex sequence was positive while all four targeted regions were absent, confirming the isolate as *M. bovis* BCG. MALDI-TOF identified the complex but could not discriminate species within it.

**解析**：使用擴增四個基因區域的multiplex PCR進行物種鑑別。Pan-complex序列陽性而四個目標區域均缺失，確認為*M. bovis* BCG。MALDI-TOF僅能識別complex層級，無法區分物種。


**Question 8. Answer: (D)**

**Explanation**: The pathogen was NOT identified preoperatively --- it was identified only after intraoperative tissue cultures grew mycobacteria. The reasons for choosing one-stage revision were: (1) the patient was elderly and frail with foot drop; (2) profound venous stasis would impair healing with repeat surgery; and (3) the indolent presentation suggested one-stage success was likely.

**解析**：病原體並非術前鑑定的——而是術中組織培養後才確認。選擇一期翻修的原因是：病人高齡體弱、嚴重靜脈鬱滯影響癒合、及亞急性病程提示一期手術成功率高。


**Question 9. Answer: (B)**

**Explanation**: *M. bovis* has intrinsic pyrazinamide resistance due to a *pncA* gene mutation, making the drug ineffective. Additionally, this patient had an elevated uric acid level at baseline, and pyrazinamide commonly causes gout flares by increasing uric acid levels --- another reason to avoid it.

**解析**：*M. bovis*因*pncA*基因突變具有固有pyrazinamide抗性，使該藥無效。此外，病人基礎尿酸偏高，pyrazinamide常因增加尿酸水平而誘發痛風，是另一個避免使用的原因。


**Question 10. Answer: (B)**

**Explanation**: The favorable 6-month outcome despite the patient's declining systemic antimycobacterial therapy is most likely attributable to aggressive surgical d\'{e}bridement with one-stage revision and the use of antibiotic-impregnated cement (vancomycin and gentamicin), which provided sufficient local infection control. The indolent nature of BCG infection and the thoroughness of the surgical d\'{e}bridement contributed to infection eradication.

**解析**：儘管病人拒絕全身性抗分枝桿菌治療，6個月良好結果最可能歸因於積極手術清創、一期翻修及含抗生素骨泥提供足夠的局部感染控制。BCG感染的亞急性特質及徹底的手術清創促成感染根除。
