## 病例呈現 (Case Presentation)


### 主訴 (Chief Complaint)


A 41-year-old man was evaluated at Massachusetts General Hospital because of **swelling in both ankles and episodes of syncope**.

一位41歲男性因**雙側腳踝腫脹及昏厥發作**至麻省總醫院就診。


### 現病史 (History of Present Illness)


The patient had been in his usual state of health until approximately **4.5 months before presentation**, when exertional dyspnea and a burning sensation in the chest developed. He was admitted to another hospital where coronary angiography showed severe stenosis of the right coronary artery, and a stent was placed. Symptoms resolved after the procedure.

病患在就診前約**4.5個月**開始出現運動性呼吸困難及胸部灼熱感。他被收入另一家醫院，冠狀動脈攝影顯示右冠狀動脈嚴重狹窄，並置入支架。術後症狀緩解。

**Ten days before current presentation**, the patient developed:


- Worsening fatigue 疲倦加劇
- Fever (38.2°C) 發燒
- Diffuse myalgia 瀰漫性肌肉痛
- Anorexia 食慾不振
- Mild headache 輕微頭痛
- New scattered ecchymoses 新發散在瘀斑
- Arthralgia in wrists and ankles 手腕及腳踝關節痛


Antigen testing for SARS-CoV-2 was negative. Symptoms abated on day 4, but on **day 5**, he experienced **two episodes of syncope**:

1. First episode: Syncope upon standing from a chair, causing a fall with right knee injury
2. Second episode: While urinating the next morning, became nauseated, diaphoretic, and lightheaded, followed by witnessed syncope


SARS-CoV-2抗原檢測陰性。症狀在第四天緩解，但在**第五天**，他經歷了**兩次昏厥發作**：第一次是從椅子站起時昏厥並跌倒導致右膝受傷；第二次是隔天早晨排尿時出現噁心、盜汗及頭暈，隨後發生目擊性昏厥。

Four days after discharge, the patient reported **new erythema and edema in both ankles**, with persistent ankle arthralgia worst in the morning and not responding to acetaminophen. On the day of admission, he had a temperature of 39.4°C and returned to the emergency department.

出院後四天，病患出現**雙側腳踝新發紅斑及水腫**，伴隨持續性關節痛，早晨最嚴重，且對acetaminophen無效。入院當天體溫達39.4°C，再次至急診就診。

## 討論 (Discussion)


### Sarcoidosis: Overview 結節病概述


Sarcoidosis is a **multisystem granulomatous disorder** of unknown etiology characterized by nonnecrotizing (noncaseating) epithelioid granulomas. It most commonly affects the lungs and intrathoracic lymph nodes but can involve virtually any organ.

結節病是一種病因不明的**多系統肉芽腫性疾病**，特徵為非壞死性（非乾酪性）類上皮肉芽腫。最常影響肺臟及胸內淋巴結，但幾乎可侵犯任何器官。


### Löfgren's Syndrome: Acute Sarcoidosis Löfgren症候群：急性結節病


First described by Swedish physician **Sven Halvar Löfgren** in 1946, this acute manifestation of sarcoidosis has distinct features:

由瑞典醫師**Sven Halvar Löfgren**於1946年首次描述，這種急性結節病表現具有獨特特徵：


| p{4cm} >{\arraybackslash}p{11cm}}  **Feature 特徵** | **Details 詳情** |  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Epidemiology 流行病學 | • More common in women and certain ethnic groups (Scandinavians, Irish)     • Typical age: 25-45 years     • Seasonal peak in spring |  |  |  |  |
| Classic Triad 經典三聯徵 | 1. Erythema nodosum 結節性紅斑     2. Bilateral hilar lymphadenopathy 雙側肺門淋巴結腫大     3. Ankle arthritis or periarthritis 踝關節炎或關節周圍炎 |  |  |  |  |
| Ankle Involvement 踝關節侵犯 | • **High specificity for diagnosis** 對診斷具高特異性     • Can be true synovitis or soft-tissue periarthritis     • Often bilateral and symmetric |  |  |  |  |

### Why Tissue Biopsy Is Not Required 為何不需組織切片


Three clinical manifestations of sarcoidosis can be diagnosed without histologic confirmation:

三種結節病臨床表現可不需組織學確認即可診斷：


1. **Löfgren's syndrome** — Erythema nodosum + bilateral hilar LAD + ankle arthritis
2. **Uveoparotid fever (Heerfordt's syndrome)** — Uveitis + parotid gland enlargement + facial palsy + fever
3. **Lupus pernio** — Violaceous skin plaques on nose, cheeks, ears

## 學習重點 (Key Takeaways)


**Clinical Pearl #1: Löfgren's Syndrome is a Clinical Diagnosis**

Unlike most sarcoidosis presentations, Löfgren's syndrome can be diagnosed **without tissue biopsy** when the classic triad is present. This is one of only three sarcoidosis presentations that don't require histologic confirmation.

與大多數結節病表現不同，當經典三聯徵存在時，Löfgren症候群可**不需組織切片**即可診斷。這是僅有的三種不需組織學確認的結節病表現之一。

**Clinical Pearl #2: Ankle Involvement Has High Specificity**

Bilateral ankle arthritis or periarthritis is **highly specific** for Löfgren's syndrome and helps distinguish it from other causes of polyarthritis with pulmonary findings.

雙側踝關節炎或關節周圍炎對Löfgren症候群具有**高特異性**，有助於與其他伴有肺部表現的多關節炎鑑別。

**Clinical Pearl #3: Excellent Prognosis**

### 答案與解析 (Answers and Explanations)


**Question 1. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: Löfgren's syndrome classically involves **bilateral, symmetric ankle arthritis or periarthritis**. Unilateral ankle involvement with dactylitis (sausage digit) is much more suggestive of **spondyloarthropathy** (such as psoriatic arthritis or reactive arthritis) rather than Löfgren's syndrome. The bilateral symmetric pattern is a key distinguishing feature that gives ankle involvement its high specificity for Löfgren's syndrome.

**解析**：Löfgren症候群經典地涉及**雙側對稱性踝關節炎或關節周圍炎**。單側踝關節侵犯伴指炎（臘腸指）更提示**脊椎關節病變**（如乾癬性關節炎或反應性關節炎）而非Löfgren症候群。雙側對稱模式是賦予踝關節侵犯對Löfgren症候群高特異性的關鍵區別特徵。


**Question 2. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: Löfgren's syndrome is one of **three clinical presentations of sarcoidosis that can be diagnosed clinically without tissue biopsy**. When the classic triad of erythema nodosum, bilateral hilar lymphadenopathy, and ankle arthritis/periarthritis is present, the diagnosis can be made on clinical grounds alone. ACE levels may be normal or elevated but are not required for diagnosis.

**解析**：Löfgren症候群是**三種可以臨床診斷而不需組織切片的結節病表現之一**。當結節性紅斑、雙側肺門淋巴結腫大及踝關節炎/關節周圍炎的經典三聯徵存在時，可僅憑臨床依據做出診斷。ACE水平可能正常或升高，但診斷不需要。


**Question 3. Answer: (D)**

**Explanation**: The combination of **cardiac MRI and 18F-FDG PET-CT provides complementary information**. Cardiac MRI has high sensitivity for detecting myocardial inflammation and fibrosis (late gadolinium enhancement) and can identify alternative diagnoses. PET-CT identifies metabolically active disease and can detect both cardiac and extracardiac sarcoidosis. Together, they offer the most comprehensive evaluation according to the HRS Expert Consensus Statement.

**解析**：**心臟MRI和18F-FDG PET-CT的組合提供互補資訊**。心臟MRI對檢測心肌發炎和纖維化（晚期釓增強）具有高敏感性，並可識別替代診斷。PET-CT識別代謝活躍的疾病，可檢測心臟和心臟外結節病。根據HRS專家共識聲明，它們共同提供最完整的評估。


**Question 4. Answer: (D)**

**Explanation**: **All features in combination** strongly argue against bartonellosis. Cat scratch disease typically causes regional lymphadenopathy near the scratch site within weeks, not systemic disease 6 months later. Bilateral hilar lymphadenopathy and symmetric oligoarthritis are both rare in CSD and would be extremely unlikely to occur simultaneously. The timing, lymph node distribution, and joint pattern are all inconsistent with CSD.

**解析**：**所有特徵的組合**強烈反對貓抓病。貓抓病通常在數週內於抓傷部位附近引起區域性淋巴結腫大，而非6個月後的全身性疾病。雙側肺門淋巴結腫大和對稱性少關節炎在CSD中都很罕見，同時發生極不可能。時機、淋巴結分布和關節模式都與CSD不符。


**Question 5. Answer: (B)**

**Explanation**: For Löfgren's syndrome that fails to respond to NSAIDs, **glucocorticoids (prednisone 20-40 mg daily with taper)** are the appropriate second-line therapy. Methotrexate and TNF inhibitors are reserved for chronic sarcoid arthropathy that develops in the minority who don't achieve remission. Biopsy is not needed if the clinical presentation is classic.

**解析**：對於NSAID無效的Löfgren症候群，**糖皮質激素（prednisone每日20-40mg漸減）**是適當的二線治療。Methotrexate和TNF抑制劑保留用於少數未達緩解者發展的慢性結節病關節病變。如果臨床表現典型，不需要切片。


**Question 6. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: **Bilateral symmetric hilar lymphadenopathy with perifissural nodules** is highly characteristic of sarcoidosis. Perifissural nodules represent a lymphatic distribution pattern typical of sarcoidosis. Unilateral mass suggests malignancy; cavitary upper lobe nodules with tree-in-bud suggest tuberculosis; calcified nodes suggest old granulomatous disease.

**解析**：**雙側對稱性肺門淋巴結腫大伴裂旁結節**是結節病的高度特徵。裂旁結節代表結節病典型的淋巴分布模式。單側腫塊提示惡性腫瘤；伴樹芽徵的上葉空洞結節提示結核病；�ite化淋巴結提示舊的肉芽腫性疾病。
