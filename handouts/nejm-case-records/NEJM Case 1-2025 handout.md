## 病例呈現 (Case Presentation)


### 主訴與現病史 (Chief Complaint & History of Present Illness)


A 35-year-old woman was admitted to this hospital because of shortness of breath and edema in the legs.

一位35歲女性因呼吸急促及下肢水腫入院。


#### 產前及生產經過 (Antepartum & Delivery)


Eight weeks before the current presentation, the patient was admitted for induction of labor at 39 weeks' gestation because of advanced maternal age and a fetus that was small for gestational age. She had begun taking low-dose aspirin at 13 weeks' gestation to decrease the risk of preeclampsia associated with antepartum obesity. She had no history of hypertension before or during pregnancy.

入院前八週，病患因高齡產婦及胎兒體重過輕，於妊娠39週時入院進行引產。她自妊娠13週起服用低劑量aspirin以降低與產前肥胖相關的子癇前症風險。病患於懷孕前及懷孕期間均無高血壓病史。

On admission for delivery:

- Blood pressure: 141/81 mm Hg（血壓：141/81 mm Hg）
- Pitting edema in legs present（下肢凹陷性水腫）
- Blood albumin: 2.1 g/dL (reference: 3.3--5.0)（血清白蛋白：2.1 g/dL，參考值：3.3--5.0）
- Urine protein/creatinine ratio: 4.1 (reference: <0.15)（尿蛋白/肌酸酐比值：4.1，參考值：<0.15）
- No headache, vision changes, or shortness of breath（無頭痛、視力改變或呼吸急促）


On the third hospital day, a healthy infant was born by uncomplicated vaginal delivery. Blood pressure after delivery was 122/88 mm Hg. She was discharged home on the fifth hospital day.

住院第三天，病患經由順利的陰道分娩產下一名健康嬰兒。產後血壓為122/88 mm Hg。於住院第五天出院返家。

## 鑑別診斷 (Differential Diagnosis)


**核心問題 (Core Clinical Question)**：  

懷孕期間新發腎病範圍蛋白尿的病因為何？  

What is the cause of new-onset nephrotic-range proteinuria in pregnancy?


**關鍵鑑別**：子癇前症（placental disease）vs. 腎絲球疾病（glomerular disease）


### 妊娠期蛋白尿鑑別診斷架構


importantbox
**妊娠相關腎臟生理變化 (Pregnancy-Related Renal Changes)**：

- 低阻力胎盤循環發育 → 平均動脈壓下降、心輸出量及血容量增加
- 腎臟血漿流量增加 → 腎絲球過濾率(GFR)上升
- 即使GFR增加，懷孕期間蛋白尿仍屬異常
- 血清白蛋白在孕期會下降，但此病患第三孕期albumin 2.0 g/dL仍屬異常偏低


importantbox

### 鑑別診斷推理過程 (Clinical Reasoning)


**為何不是子癇前症？**


1. **血壓表現不典型**：子癇前症通常有更持續且嚴重的高血壓，此病患僅有一次輕度升高
2. **產後病程**：子癇前症的蛋白尿應在產後6週內完全緩解，此病患產後8週仍有持續的腎病範圍蛋白尿
3. **若未緩解**：通常是非腎病範圍且來自嚴重早產型子癇前症的殘餘腎臟損傷


importantbox
**sFlt-1:PlGF 比值的診斷價值**：


- 子癇前症源於血管新生失衡：病態胎盤釋放過量sFlt-1（可溶性VEGF受體）
- sFlt-1結合VEGF及PlGF → 廣泛內皮功能障礙
- **高sFlt-1:PlGF比值**：支持子癇前症
- **低sFlt-1:PlGF比值**：建議考慮其他腎絲球疾病
- 此生物標記已在歐洲及加拿大使用近十年，近期獲FDA核准於美國使用

### 治療反應與復發 (Treatment Response & Relapse)


**初始反應（治療4週後）**：

- 達到**部分緩解**（partial remission）
- 尿蛋白：6.2 g/day → 2.7 g/day

**緩解定義**：

- **完全緩解**：尿蛋白<0.3 g/day + 穩定Cr + Albumin>3.5 g/dL
- **部分緩解**：蛋白尿顯著下降但未達完全緩解標準

## 學習重點 (Key Takeaways)


1. **Clinical Pearl 1**: 懷孕期間新發蛋白尿需區分子癇前症（placental disease）與腎絲球疾病（glomerular disease），因處置策略截然不同。          *New-onset proteinuria in pregnancy requires differentiation between preeclampsia and glomerular diseases, as management differs substantially.*
2. **Clinical Pearl 2**: 子癇前症的蛋白尿通常在產後6週內完全緩解。持續的腎病範圍蛋白尿應考慮其他腎絲球疾病。          *Proteinuria from preeclampsia typically resolves within 6 weeks postpartum. Persistent nephrotic-range proteinuria suggests an underlying glomerular disease.*
3. **Clinical Pearl 3**: sFlt-1:PlGF比值可協助鑑別子癇前症與其他腎絲球疾病，尤其在臨床表現不典型且胎兒早產時特別有用。          *The sFlt-1:PlGF ratio can help distinguish preeclampsia from other glomerular diseases, particularly useful when clinical features are inconclusive and the fetus is preterm.*
4. **Clinical Pearl 4**: 原發性FSGS以廣泛、全面的足細胞足突消失為特徵，腎臟切片是確診的關鍵。          *Primary FSGS is characterized by diffuse and global podocyte foot-process effacement; kidney biopsy is essential for diagnosis.*
5. **Clinical Pearl 5**: 原發性FSGS合併腎病症候群的治療以免疫抑制為主，高劑量glucocorticoids為第一線。超過50          *Treatment of primary FSGS with nephrotic syndrome centers on immunosuppression, with high-dose glucocorticoids as first-line. More than 50
6. **Clinical Pearl 6**: 類固醇依賴型FSGS可考慮rituximab，一種anti-CD20單株抗體，已顯示對此病患群的療效。          *For glucocorticoid-dependent FSGS, rituximab (anti-CD20 monoclonal antibody) has shown efficacy and can be considered as an alternative or adjunctive therapy.*

產後持續或惡化的腎病範圍蛋白尿 $\neq$ 子癇前症殘餘表現


**Persistent or worsening nephrotic-range proteinuria postpartum is NOT simply residual preeclampsia!**


必須考慮原發性腎絲球疾病並安排腎臟切片！
