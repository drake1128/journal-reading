## 討論 (Discussion)


### Cardiac Tamponade 心包填塞的病理生理學


- Intrapericardial pressure $\geq$ right atrial pressure $\Rightarrow$ impaired early diastolic RV filling
- Y descent blunted or absent; stroke volume reduced; blood pressure decreases
- **Ventricular interdependence**: During inspiration, RV fills at expense of LV $\Rightarrow$ **pulsus paradoxus**（吸氣時SBP下降$\geq$10 mm Hg）
- Pericardial compliance and rate of fluid accumulation determine clinical course:     itemize
- Rapid bleed of 150--250 mL $\rightarrow$ acute tamponade（急性填塞）
- Slow malignant effusion $\rightarrow$ may reach several hundred mL before compromise（慢性積液可容許較大量）


    \item Pulsus paradoxus $>$12 mm Hg: sensitivity 98
itemize


### Acute Aortic Syndromes 急性主動脈症候群


Acute aortic syndromes include: dissection, intramural hematoma, penetrating ulcer, transection, and abrupt aneurysm expansion with threatened or contained rupture.

急性主動脈症候群包括：剝離、壁內血腫、穿透性潰瘍、橫斷及突發性動脈瘤擴張伴威脅性或局限性破裂。


- Involvement of aortic root and proximal ascending aorta (within pericardial reflection) $\rightarrow$ rapid hemopericardium $\rightarrow$ tamponade $\rightarrow$ syncope and death
- **Pericardiocentesis is only a temporizing measure**: risk of death from ongoing aortic-to-pericardial flow remains high
- **Definitive management: Urgent cardiac surgery**（確定治療：緊急心臟手術）


### Fungal Endocarditis 真菌性心內膜炎


importantbox
**Key Findings from Surgery and Pathology 手術及病理關鍵發現**


- **Intraoperative TEE**: Vegetation on noncoronary aortic-valve leaflet; aortic pseudoaneurysm 21$\times$16 mm; thickened anterior mitral-valve leaflet; mild aortic regurgitation
- **Surgical findings**: Diffuse fibrinous material around heart; thickened, inflamed aorta; abscess cavity in noncoronary sinus wall
- **Pathology**: Mixed acute and chronic inflammation in aortic and mitral valves; **narrow, septate fungal hyphae with acute-angle branching**（GMS stain）$\Rightarrow$ morphologically consistent with *Aspergillus* species
- **Aortic wall**: Intimal thickening, neutrophilic infiltration of media, adventitial abscess $\Rightarrow$ **acute suppurative aortitis**
- **Culture**: ***Aspergillus flavus***


importantbox


### Aspergillus Endocarditis的臨床特徵


- *Aspergillus* endocarditis is a rare but devastating form of fungal endocarditis
- Risk factors: IV drug use, prosthetic valves, immunosuppression, prolonged antibiotic therapy
- Blood cultures are **frequently negative**（血液培養經常陰性）
- Gram's stain of pericardial fluid will **not detect fungi**（革蘭氏染色無法偵測真菌）
- Characteristically causes **large vegetations** with propensity for embolization
- Can cause **abscess formation and tissue destruction**$\rightarrow$ aortic-root pseudoaneurysm $\rightarrow$ contained rupture $\rightarrow$ hemopericardium
- Mortality rate is extremely high


**Retrospective Clue 回顧性線索**

6週前入院時TTE即發現mitral-valve leaflet thickening和noncoronary aortic-valve leaflet thickening，但當時血液培養陰性、經驗性抗生素治療後改善出院。回顧性來看，這些瓣膜增厚很可能已是*Aspergillus* endocarditis的早期表現，而抗生素對真菌無效——病情在6週後以catastrophic aortic rupture呈現。


### Opioid Withdrawal管理對臨床決策的影響


- 病人因嚴重opioid withdrawal疼痛，**多次拒絕接受進一步檢查**（CT angiography）
- Addiction medicine team介入；先控制withdrawal symptoms再進行診斷檢查
- 使用methadone（繼續門診劑量）及short-acting opioids for pain
- Benzodiazepine withdrawal以CIWA-Ar（原為alcohol withdrawal設計）作為proxy assessment tool
- 此案例突顯：**substance use disorder的管理是急性心臟病照護中不可忽視的一環**

### Postoperative Course and Outcome 術後病程及結果


- Diffuse coagulopathy with increased chest tube output and multipressor shock
- Bedside echocardiography: RV failure with fluid accumulation and chamber compression
- Emergency re-exploration with pericardial packing (chest left open)
- Postoperative days 3--4: Two additional re-explorations for ongoing coagulopathy and bleeding
- Progressive liver and kidney failure with persistent lactic acidosis despite broad-spectrum antimicrobials
- **Goals of care transitioned to comfort measures only; patient died 8 days after initial presentation**


**Critical Point 關鍵要點**: Fungal endocarditis carries an extremely high mortality rate. *Aspergillus* endocarditis is particularly devastating due to its tendency for tissue invasion, abscess formation, and embolization. Even with prompt surgical intervention and antimicrobial therapy, outcomes remain poor.

真菌性心內膜炎死亡率極高。*Aspergillus*心內膜炎因其組織侵犯、膿瘍形成及栓塞傾向而特別致命。即使及時手術及抗微生物治療，預後仍不佳。


## 學習重點 (Key Takeaways)


**1. Beck's Triad and Cardiac Tamponade 貝克三聯症與心包填塞**

- Beck's triad (hypotension, elevated JVP, distant heart sounds) is highly specific for cardiac tamponade
- Pulsus paradoxus $>$12 mm Hg has 98
- Point-of-care ultrasound is more sensitive than physical examination for detecting pericardial effusion
- 心包填塞是臨床診斷，輔以超音波確認，需緊急pericardiocentesis


**2. Hemorrhagic Pericardial Effusion: Think Beyond Pericarditis 血性心包積液不只是心包炎**

- Hemorrhagic effusion with continued high-volume drainage $\Rightarrow$ consider aortic rupture, not just pericarditis
- Acute aortic syndromes involving the aortic root can cause rapid hemopericardium
- Pericardiocentesis is only a temporizing measure; definitive treatment requires surgery
- 持續大量血性引流提示活動性出血來源，需緊急CT angiography


**3. Fungal Endocarditis in Persons Who Inject Drugs 注射藥物使用者的真菌性心內膜炎**

- *Aspergillus* endocarditis is rare but has extremely high mortality
- Blood cultures and Gram's stain are often negative for fungal pathogens
- Valve thickening on echocardiography without positive cultures should raise suspicion for fungal etiology
- 真菌性心內膜炎的血液培養及革蘭氏染色常為陰性，echo上的瓣膜增厚應提高懷疑


**4. Importance of Revisiting Prior Imaging Findings 回顧先前影像發現的重要性**

- This patient's TTE 6 weeks earlier showed valve thickening --- a retrospective clue to endocarditis
- Negative blood cultures at that time may have led to premature reassurance
- In high-risk patients (IVDU), unexplained valve abnormalities warrant aggressive follow-up
- 高風險病人（IVDU）的不明原因瓣膜異常需積極追蹤，不應因培養陰性而放鬆警惕


**5. Substance Use Disorder and Acute Cardiac Care 藥物使用疾患與急性心臟照護**

- Opioid and benzodiazepine withdrawal can significantly impede diagnostic workup
- This patient declined CT angiography until withdrawal symptoms were managed
- Addiction medicine consultation is essential for co-management in acute cardiac emergencies
- 藥物戒斷症狀的管理是急性心臟病照護中不可忽視的一環，需多專科團隊合作

### 答案與解析 (Answers and Explanations)


**Question 1. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: A structured review of eight studies showed that pulsus paradoxus greater than 12 mm Hg has 98

**解析**：系統性回顧顯示pulsus paradoxus $>$12 mm Hg對心包填塞的敏感度98


**Question 2. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: In cardiac tamponade, ventricular interdependence is the key mechanism. During inspiration, the right ventricle fills at the expense of the left ventricle because both ventricles share a fixed total volume within the non-compliant pericardium. This results in decreased left ventricular stroke volume and a fall in systolic blood pressure during inspiration (pulsus paradoxus).

**解析**：心包填塞中，ventricular interdependence是關鍵機制。吸氣時右心室充盈增加，在不可擴張的心包內擠壓左心室，導致左心室搏出量下降及吸氣時收縮壓下降（pulsus paradoxus）。


**Question 3. Answer: (B)**

**Explanation**: A brisk bleed of 150 to 250 mL into the pericardial space may result in tamponade when pericardial compliance is exceeded. In contrast, slow-accumulating effusions (e.g., malignant effusions) may reach several hundred milliliters before causing hemodynamic compromise, because the pericardium gradually stretches.

**解析**：快速出血150--250 mL即可超過心包順應性而導致填塞。相反地，緩慢累積的積液可在數百毫升後才造成血行動力學障礙，因心包可漸進性擴張。


**Question 4. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: Continued high-volume bloody drainage after pericardiocentesis suggests an ongoing source of active bleeding into the pericardial space. In this clinical context, acute aortic rupture (from dissection, pseudoaneurysm, or contained rupture) is the most urgent diagnosis to consider, as it requires emergent surgical intervention.

**解析**：心包穿刺後持續大量血性引流提示持續活動性出血來源。在此臨床情境下，急性主動脈破裂是最需緊急考慮的診斷，因其需要緊急手術介入。


**Question 5. Answer: (D)**

**Explanation**: All three components of Beck's triad were present in this patient: hypotension (BP 79/61 mm Hg), elevated jugular venous pressure (JVP 15 cm H$_2$O), and distant heart sounds. Note that pericardial friction rub is NOT part of Beck's triad; it is a sign of pericarditis rather than tamponade.

**解析**：此病人具備Beck's triad全部三項：低血壓（79/61 mm Hg）、頸靜脈壓升高（15 cm H$_2$O）及心音遙遠。心包摩擦音不是Beck's triad的一部分，它是心包炎而非填塞的徵象。


**Question 6. Answer: (D)**

**Explanation**: Pericardiocentesis is only a temporizing measure for hemopericardium due to aortic rupture. The risk of death from ongoing aortic-to-pericardial flow remains high even after drainage. Definitive management requires urgent cardiac surgery with repair of the affected segment of the aorta.

**解析**：心包穿刺引流對主動脈破裂致血心包僅為暫時性措施。即使引流後，持續主動脈至心包的血流仍有高死亡風險。確定治療需緊急心臟手術修復受影響的主動脈節段。


**Question 7. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: Gram's stain is designed to detect bacteria based on their cell wall properties. Fungi such as *Aspergillus* species are not reliably detected by Gram's stain and require specialized staining techniques (e.g., Grocott's methenamine--silver stain or calcofluor white) or fungal cultures for identification.

**解析**：革蘭氏染色針對細菌細胞壁特性設計。真菌如*Aspergillus*無法被革蘭氏染色可靠偵測，需要特殊染色技術（如GMS染色或calcofluor white）或真菌培養來識別。


**Question 8. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: Grocott's methenamine--silver (GMS) stain was used in this case to identify fungal hyphae in the resected aortic valve, mitral valve, and aortic wall tissue. GMS stain highlights fungal cell walls in black against a green background and is the standard histologic stain for identifying fungi in tissue specimens.

**解析**：本案使用Grocott's methenamine--silver（GMS）染色在切除的主動脈瓣、二尖瓣及主動脈壁組織中識別真菌菌絲。GMS染色將真菌細胞壁染成黑色（綠色背景），是組織檢體中識別真菌的標準組織學染色。


**Question 9. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: The description of narrow, septate hyphae with acute-angle (45-degree) branching is the classic morphologic pattern of *Aspergillus* species. This contrasts with Mucor species, which show broad, ribbon-like, pauciseptate hyphae with right-angle (90-degree) branching. The culture confirmed *Aspergillus flavus*.

**解析**：窄、有隔膜、銳角（45度）分枝的菌絲是*Aspergillus*的經典形態。相比之下，Mucor呈現寬、帶狀、少隔膜、直角（90度）分枝。培養確認為*Aspergillus flavus*。


**Question 10. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: Thickening of the mitral-valve leaflets and the noncoronary aortic-valve leaflet on TTE 6 weeks earlier was a retrospective clue to endocarditis. In patients with risk factors for endocarditis (such as injection drug use), unexplained valve abnormalities should prompt aggressive follow-up even when blood cultures are negative. Negative cultures do not rule out fungal endocarditis, as *Aspergillus* species rarely grow in routine blood cultures.

**解析**：6週前TTE上的二尖瓣葉及非冠狀動脈主動脈瓣葉增厚是心內膜炎的回顧性線索。在有心內膜炎危險因子的病人（如注射藥物使用者）中，不明原因的瓣膜異常應積極追蹤，即使血液培養陰性。培養陰性不能排除真菌性心內膜炎，因*Aspergillus*在常規血液培養中很少生長。
