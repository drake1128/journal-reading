## 討論


### Lyme Disease概述


> **病原體**: *Borrelia burgdorferi*(螺旋體)
> **傳播媒介**: *Ixodes scapularis*(黑腳蜱/鹿蜱)
> **流行地區**: 美國東北部、中西部上區、太平洋沿岸
> **高峰期**: 5-7月(nymphal tick活動期)


### Lyme Disease的臨床分期


| p{4cm} p{8cm}}  **Stage** | **Timing** | **Clinical Manifestations** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Stage 1**    Localized infection | Days to weeks after tick bite | - **Erythema migrans (EM)**: 80  - Flulike symptoms: fatigue, myalgia, headache    - 20 |
| **Stage 2**    Early disseminated | Days to weeks after onset | - **Multiple EM lesions**(dissemination marker)    - **Neuroborreliosis**: lymphocytic meningitis, cranial neuritis, radiculopathy    - **Carditis**: AV block    - Intermittent joint pain |
| **Stage 3**    Persistent infection | Months later | - **Lyme arthritis**: 大關節(特別是膝關節)    - Late neurologic manifestations |


### Lyme Neuroborreliosis

1. **Lymphocytic meningitis**(淋巴球性腦膜炎)
2. **Cranial neuropathy**(顱神經病變) — 最常見CN VII,其次CN III
3. **Painful radiculitis**(疼痛性神經根炎)

### Bannwarth's Syndrome


> 一種Lyme neuroborreliosis的subset,在歐洲比美國更常見。
> **Classic Triad:**
> enumerate
>     \item **Severe painful radiculopathy**(hallmark of the syndrome)
>     \item **CSF lymphocytic pleocytosis**
>     \item **Cranial neuritis**(可能multiple)
> enumerate
> **歐洲 vs 美國:**
> itemize
>     \item 歐洲: 主要由*B. garinii*引起,radiculopathy更prominent
>     \item 美國: *B. burgdorferi*引起,meningeal involvement with severe headache更常見,facial palsy常見但radiculopathy較少
> itemize
> 此病人的presentation(radiculopathy followed by cranial neuritis)符合Bannwarth's syndrome。


### Glucocorticoids對Lyme Disease的影響


**重要臨床考量**

此病人在術後第3天開始接受prednisone,隨後又接受dexamethasone。根據動物研究:

- Glucocorticoids會impair對螺旋體的免疫反應
- 導致更高的microbial burden
- 造成更嚴重的疾病表現


這可能解釋了此病人relatively severe presentation。

### Lyme Disease的血清學診斷


| p{11cm}}  **Method** | **Description** |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Standard Two-Tiered Testing (STTT)** | **Step 1**: EIA測量IgM和IgG對*B. burgdorferi*的反應    **Step 2**: Equivocal或positive時,進行Western blot確認     **注意**: Positive IgM alone僅在感染第一個月內有意義;之後應有IgG反應 |  |

> **Result**: **Positive IgM and IgG responses to *B. burgdorferi***

## 學習重點 (Key Takeaways)


**1. Lyme Neuroborreliosis的臨床表現**

- Classic triad: lymphocytic meningitis + cranial neuropathy + painful radiculitis
- Cranial nerve VII最常受影響,其次CN III
- MRI可見multiple cranial nerve enhancement
- **美國東北部在夏季就診的cranial neuropathy,務必考慮Lyme disease**


**2. Bannwarth's Syndrome**

- Lyme neuroborreliosis的一種表現
- Triad: severe painful radiculopathy + CSF pleocytosis + cranial neuritis
- 歐洲較常見,但美國也有報告
- Radicular pain是hallmark of the syndrome


**3. 診斷方法**

- Standard or modified two-tiered serologic testing
- Positive IgM alone僅在第一個月有意義
- CSF testing需計算CSF-serum antibody index
- Western blot可提供additional information


**4. Coinfection的重要性**

- 2-10
- 持續fever是重要線索
- *I. scapularis*可傳播: Babesia, Anaplasma, *B. miyamotoi*, Powassan virus
- 對治療反應不佳時要考慮coinfection


**5. Glucocorticoids的影響**

- 可impair對螺旋體及coinfecting agents的免疫反應
- 導致higher microbial burden及more severe disease
- 可能加重babesiosis(對glucocorticoids具permissive effect)
- **術後腰痛惡化時,不要輕易使用steroids而忽略感染可能**

### 答案與解析 (Answers and Explanations)


**Question 1. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: The combination of geographic location (Connecticut, endemic for Lyme disease), timing (July, peak month for Lyme neuroborreliosis), and clinical triad of headache, cranial neuropathy (facial weakness), and radicular pain is classic for Lyme neuroborreliosis. Multiple cranial nerve enhancement on MRI supports this diagnosis.

**解析**：地理位置(Connecticut為Lyme disease流行區)、就診時間(七月為Lyme neuroborreliosis的高峰月份)、以及頭痛、顱神經病變(顏面無力)和神經根痛的臨床三聯症,都是Lyme neuroborreliosis的經典表現。MRI上多條顱神經增強支持此診斷。


**Question 2. Answer: (D)**

**Explanation**: Cranial nerve VII (facial nerve) is the most commonly affected cranial nerve in Lyme neuroborreliosis, followed by cranial nerve III. Facial palsy (often bilateral) is the most common manifestation of Lyme neuroborreliosis in the United States.

**解析**：第七顱神經(顏面神經)是Lyme neuroborreliosis最常受影響的顱神經,其次是第三顱神經。顏面神經麻痺(常為雙側)是美國Lyme neuroborreliosis最常見的表現。


**Question 3. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: Persistent fever despite appropriate antibiotic treatment for Lyme disease is an important clue for coinfection. *Ixodes scapularis* ticks can transmit multiple pathogens including *Babesia microti* and *Anaplasma phagocytophilum*. The case presented in the article specifically highlights this point.

**解析**：儘管接受適當抗生素治療但仍持續發燒,是coinfection的重要線索。*Ixodes scapularis*蜱可傳播多種病原體,包括*Babesia microti*和*Anaplasma phagocytophilum*。本文中的病例特別強調了這一點。


**Question 4. Answer: (D)**

**Explanation**: Bannwarth's syndrome is characterized by the triad of painful radiculopathy (hallmark), CSF lymphocytic pleocytosis, and cranial neuritis. Bilateral lower extremity weakness is NOT a characteristic feature. This syndrome is a subset of Lyme neuroborreliosis, more common in Europe than the United States.

**解析**：Bannwarth's syndrome的特徵為三聯症：疼痛性神經根病變(hallmark)、CSF淋巴球增多、顱神經炎。雙側下肢無力並非其特徵。此症候群是Lyme neuroborreliosis的一種subset,在歐洲比美國更常見。


**Question 5. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: A positive IgM response alone should only be used to support the diagnosis of Lyme disease during the first month of infection. After that time, patients usually have an IgG response, which is more specific than an IgM response. CSF testing requires concurrent serum samples to calculate the CSF-serum antibody index.

**解析**：單獨的IgM陽性反應僅應在感染後第一個月內用於支持Lyme disease的診斷。之後,病人通常會出現IgG反應,其特異性高於IgM反應。CSF檢測需要同時收集血清樣本以計算CSF-serum antibody index。


**Question 6. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: Based on animal studies, glucocorticoids impair immune responses to the spirochete and to coinfecting agents, leading to greater microbial burden and more severe disease. This is specifically mentioned in the case as a potential contributor to the patient's relatively severe presentation.

**解析**：根據動物研究,glucocorticoids會損害對螺旋體和共同感染病原體的免疫反應,導致更高的微生物負荷和更嚴重的疾病。本病例特別提到這可能是病人表現相對嚴重的原因之一。


**Question 7. Answer: (B)**

**Explanation**: *Ixodes scapularis* can transmit multiple pathogens including *Borrelia burgdorferi* (Lyme disease), *Babesia microti*, *Anaplasma phagocytophilum*, *Borrelia miyamotoi*, and Powassan virus. *Rickettsia rickettsii* (Rocky Mountain spotted fever) is transmitted by different tick species.

**解析**：*Ixodes scapularis*可傳播多種病原體,包括*Borrelia burgdorferi*(Lyme disease)、*Babesia microti*、*Anaplasma phagocytophilum*、*Borrelia miyamotoi*和Powassan virus。*Rickettsia rickettsii*(Rocky Mountain spotted fever)則由不同種類的蜱傳播。


**Question 8. Answer: (C)**

**Explanation**: According to IDSA/AAN/ACR 2020 guidelines, appropriate treatment regimens for Lyme meningitis, cranial neuropathy, or radiculoneuropathy include IV ceftriaxone, IV penicillin, or oral doxycycline for 14-21 days. Studies have shown oral doxycycline is non-inferior to IV ceftriaxone for European Lyme neuroborreliosis.

**解析**：根據IDSA/AAN/ACR 2020指引,Lyme meningitis、cranial neuropathy或radiculoneuropathy的適當治療方案包括IV ceftriaxone、IV penicillin或oral doxycycline,療程14-21天。研究顯示oral doxycycline對歐洲Lyme neuroborreliosis的療效不劣於IV ceftriaxone。


**Question 9. Answer: (B)**

**Explanation**: Concurrent leptomeningeal enhancement is more suggestive of neurosarcoidosis. The original article states: "The absence of concurrent leptomeningeal enhancement also decreases the likelihood of neurosarcoidosis." Lyme neuroborreliosis typically shows cranial nerve enhancement without prominent leptomeningeal involvement.

**解析**：同時出現的軟腦膜增強更提示neurosarcoidosis。原文指出：「缺乏同時出現的軟腦膜增強也降低了neurosarcoidosis的可能性。」Lyme neuroborreliosis通常顯示顱神經增強但無明顯軟腦膜侵犯。
