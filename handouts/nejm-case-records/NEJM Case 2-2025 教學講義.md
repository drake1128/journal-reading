## 病例呈現 (Case Presentation)


### 主訴與現病史 (Chief Complaint & History of Present Illness)


A 21-year-old man with sickle cell disease was evaluated in the emergency department of this hospital after loss of consciousness resulting in a fall.

一位患有鐮刀型紅血球疾病的21歲男性，因意識喪失導致跌倒，於本院急診室接受評估。


The patient had been in his usual state of health until the week before the current presentation, when he began to have discomfort and pain in the chest, back, arms, and legs. He attributed these symptoms to sickle cell disease and received hydromorphone infusions on an outpatient basis 4 days and 3 days before this evaluation.

病患於就診前一週健康狀況尚可，之後開始出現胸部、背部、手臂及雙腿不適與疼痛。他將這些症狀歸因於鐮刀型紅血球疾病，並於就診前4天及3天以門診方式接受hydromorphone輸注治療。


On the day of the current presentation, the patient used oral oxycodone and supplemental oxygen for chest pain. In the evening, his family was downstairs when they heard a thud from upstairs; they found the patient lying on the floor of his bedroom. There was no evidence of limb shaking or incontinence. The patient awoke in response to voice but was described as being confused for approximately 1 minute before returning to his baseline mental status.

就診當天，病患因胸痛使用口服oxycodone及氧氣補充。傍晚，家人在樓下聽到樓上傳來碰撞聲，發現病患躺在臥室地板上。無肢體抽搐或尿失禁的證據。病患對聲音有反應並清醒，但被描述為意識混亂約1分鐘後恢復至基線精神狀態。


He recalled taking a nap and then feeling dizzy on awakening. The last thing he remembered was trying to walk across his room. Emergency medical services were activated; the fingerstick blood glucose level was normal, and the Glasgow Coma Scale score was 15.

他回憶起自己正在小睡，醒來時感到頭暈。他最後記得的是試圖走過房間。緊急醫療服務被啟動；指尖血糖正常，Glasgow昏迷指數為15分。


### 系統回顧 (Review of Systems)


In the emergency department, a review of systems was notable for head and face pain on the right side from the fall, as well as acute-on-chronic pain involving the chest, back, and right hip and leg that was similar to the patient's previous sickle cell--related pain. He reported no fever, nausea, vomiting, change in bowel habits, dyspnea, rash, weakness, or numbness.

在急診室，系統回顧顯示跌倒後右側頭面部疼痛，以及胸部、背部、右髖及右腿的急性加重慢性疼痛，與病患先前鐮刀型紅血球疾病相關疼痛相似。他否認發燒、噁心、嘔吐、排便習慣改變、呼吸困難、皮疹、無力或麻木。

### 住院後癲癇發作 (In-Hospital Seizure)


Six hours after presentation, on the second hospital day, the patient was witnessed to have an abrupt onset of shaking of the arms and legs with flexion of the arms. The eyes were deviated upward. The event lasted approximately 2 minutes and stopped on its own; intravenous lorazepam was administered 7 minutes after the seizure. The patient was sleepy but his condition was otherwise unchanged.

就診後6小時（第二住院日），病患被目擊突然出現雙上肢及雙下肢抖動，伴隨上肢屈曲。眼球向上偏斜。發作持續約2分鐘後自行停止；癲癇發作後7分鐘給予靜脈lorazepam。病患嗜睡，但其餘狀況與先前檢查無異。

## 討論 (Discussion)


### Posterior Reversible Encephalopathy Syndrome (PRES) 後部可逆性腦病變症候群


#### 病理生理機轉 (Pathophysiology)


PRES results from dysfunction of cerebral autoregulation, leading to hyperperfusion and breakdown of the blood-brain barrier. The posterior cerebral circulation is preferentially affected due to relatively sparse sympathetic innervation.

PRES源於腦血管自動調節功能障礙，導致過度灌流及血腦障壁破壞。後循環因交感神經支配相對稀疏而優先受累。


**Key mechanisms 主要機轉**：

- **Endothelial dysfunction 內皮功能障礙**：Central to PRES pathophysiology; sickle cell disease is strongly associated with endothelial dysfunction
- **Hypertension 高血壓**：Commonly associated but not required; cases without hypertension have been described
- **Cerebral autoregulation failure 腦血管自動調節失敗**：Leads to hyperperfusion injury
- **Blood-brain barrier breakdown 血腦障壁破壞**：Results in vasogenic edema

### 鐮刀型紅血球疾病的神經學併發症 (Neurological Complications in SCD)

## 學習重點 (Key Takeaways)


1. **Clinical Pearl 1**: In SCD patients presenting with neurologic symptoms, PRES is an important mimic of stroke that should be considered early.          *臨床要點一*：SCD病患出現神經學症狀時，PRES是重要的中風模仿者，應早期列入考慮。
2. **Clinical Pearl 2**: The MRI hallmark of PRES is symmetric posterior-predominant vasogenic edema on FLAIR imaging without restricted diffusion on DWI.          *臨床要點二*：PRES的MRI特徵為FLAIR上對稱性後方為主的血管性水腫，DWI無受限擴散。
3. **Clinical Pearl 3**: ASH guidelines recommend prompt red-cell exchange for SCD patients with focal neurologic symptoms without requiring identification of etiology first.          *臨床要點三*：ASH指引建議具有局部神經學症狀的SCD病患緊急紅血球交換，不需先確認病因。
4. **Clinical Pearl 4**: Seizure is 2-3 times more common in SCD patients; consider multiple etiologies including PRES, stroke, fat embolism, and underlying epilepsy.          *臨床要點四*：SCD病患的癲癇發生率是一般人群的2-3倍；需考慮多種病因包括PRES、中風、脂肪栓塞及潛在癲癇。
5. **Clinical Pearl 5**: SWI microhemorrhages in SCD patients may reflect chronic sequelae of previous fat embolism rather than acute pathology.          *臨床要點五*：SCD病患的SWI微出血灶可能反映先前脂肪栓塞的慢性後遺症而非急性病理變化。
6. **Clinical Pearl 6**: PRES can occur without classic features (visual changes, severe hypertension, nausea/vomiting); imaging is key to diagnosis.          *臨床要點六*：PRES可在缺乏典型特徵（視覺改變、嚴重高血壓、噁心嘔吐）下發生；影像學是診斷關鍵。


importantbox
**Red Flags in SCD Patients（鐮刀型紅血球疾病患者的危險徵兆）**：


- New focal neurologic symptoms → Urgent red-cell exchange    新發局部神經學症狀 → 緊急紅血球交換
- Seizure in SCD → Consider stroke, PRES, fat embolism    SCD病患癲癇 → 考慮中風、PRES、脂肪栓塞
- Respiratory symptoms + neurologic changes → Fat embolism syndrome    呼吸症狀合併神經學變化 → 脂肪栓塞症候群
- Severe pain crisis + altered mental status → Urgent neuroimaging    嚴重疼痛危象合併意識改變 → 緊急神經影像檢查
