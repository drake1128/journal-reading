# 研究背景

---

# IVUS 於 PCI 的角色

- **IVUS** 提供 high-resolution tomographic imaging
  - Lesion morphology assessment
  - Stent sizing and selection
  - Post-procedural stent expansion / apposition evaluation
- 先前 RCT + meta-analyses 顯示 IVUS-guided PCI 降低 **MACE**
  - 主要驅動因素：減少 repeat revascularization
  - 多數證據來自 **亞洲（韓國）** 試驗
- **ESC 2024 Guidelines**：intracoronary imaging for complex PCI = **Class Ia**

> **關鍵問題**：在歐洲高量中心，IVUS 的 incremental benefit 是否仍然存在？

---

# 未解決的問題

- 缺乏大規模 RCT 專門針對 **left main PCI** 比較 IVUS vs. angiography
- RENOVATE-COMPLEX-PCI（韓國）的結果能否推廣至 **歐洲族群**？
- 在 contemporary stent platform + 嚴格 procedural standards 下，IVUS 是否仍有 incremental benefit？
- 西方國家 intracoronary imaging adoption 仍偏低（< 30%）

---

# OPTIMAL 試驗

---

# OPTIMAL — 試驗設計

## Optimization of Left Main PCI with Intravascular Ultrasound

| 項目 | 內容 |
|------|------|
| 類型 | International, multicenter, open-label, randomized, superiority |
| 對象 | Unprotected left main coronary artery disease |
| 樣本數 | **806** (401 vs. 405) |
| 中心 | 28 centers (Italy, Spain, UK) |
| 隨機 | 1:1, stratified by center |
| Stent | Synergy / Synergy Megatron (Boston Scientific) |
| Primary endpoint | POCE: stroke + MI + any revasc + death |
| Follow-up | Median **2.9 years** |

---

# OPTIMAL — 收案條件與 IVUS Criteria

## 收案條件
- Unprotected left main stenosis ≥50%
- Heart team / interventional cardiologist 評估適合 PCI
- 中至重度 ischemia 或有症狀

## IVUS Optimization Criteria

| 位置 | Minimum Lumen Area |
|------|-------------------|
| Left main body | ≥ 8 mm² |
| Polygon of confluence | ≥ 7 mm² |
| Proximal LAD / LCx | ≥ 6 mm² |
| Proximal LCx | ≥ 5 mm² |

- Minimal malapposition + no substantial edge dissection

---

# OPTIMAL — 基線特徵

| 特徵 | IVUS (N=401) | Angio (N=405) |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| Age (yrs) | 71.1 ± 11.0 | 71.7 ± 10.4 |
| Male | 78.6% | 78.3% |
| NSTEMI | 39.5% | 38.8% |
| Diabetes | 36.6% | 32.8% |
| Previous heart failure | 25.8% | 19.8% |
| LVEF (%) | 53.2 ± 11.1 | 53.4 ± 10.4 |
| **SYNTAX score** | **30.1 ± 13.0** | **29.3 ± 12.1** |
| 3-vessel disease | 58.0% | 57.8% |

> **Pearl**: Mean SYNTAX 29.7 — 高於 EXCEL (26.5) 和 NOBLE (22.5)，反映 real-world complexity

---

# OPTIMAL — Procedural Data

| 特徵 | IVUS-Guided | Angio-Guided |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| IVUS at any time | **97.5%** | 3.0% |
| Post-stent dilation | 97.0% | **96.0%** |
| POT | 89.6% | **85.1%** |
| Stents/patient | 2.48 | 2.40 |
| Total stent length (mm) | 58.7 | 57.6 |
| Max stent diameter (mm) | 3.71 | 3.66 |
| **Procedure time (min)** | **88.6** | **63.9** |
| Complications | 7.4% | 7.2% |

> **Pearl**: Angio 組 post-stent dilation **96%**、POT **85%** — 極高 procedural standards！

---

# OPTIMAL — 主要結果

## Primary Endpoint: POCE at median 2.9 years

| 終點 | IVUS | Angio | HR (95% CI) |
|------|------|-------|-------------|
| **Primary POCE** | **33.7%** | **30.9%** | **1.11 (0.87–1.42), P=0.40** |
| DOCE | 22.4% | 20.5% | 1.10 (0.82–1.49) |
| VOCE | 24.2% | 21.5% | 1.14 (0.85–1.52) |
| All-cause death | 15.7% | 15.1% | 1.06 (0.74–1.50) |
| Stroke | 3.0% | 1.0% | 3.11 (1.00–9.65) |
| MI | 11.2% | 10.9% | 1.04 (0.68–1.57) |
| TLR | 8.0% | 8.9% | 0.91 (0.56–1.46) |

> **結論**：IVUS-guided PCI **未優於** angiography-guided PCI

# IVUS-CHIP — 試驗設計

## IVUS Guidance for Complex High-Risk Indicated Procedures

| 項目 | 內容 |
|------|------|
| 類型 | International, multicenter, open-label, event-driven, randomized |
| 對象 | Complex high-risk PCI (≥1 complex lesion 或 planned MCS) |
| 樣本數 | **2019** (1010 vs. 1009) |
| 中心 | 37 centers, 7 European countries |
| 隨機 | 1:1, stratified by site + clinical presentation |
| Stent | Synergy / Synergy Megatron (Boston Scientific) |
| Primary endpoint | TVF: cardiac death + TV-MI + CI-TVR |
| Follow-up | Median **19.0 months** |

---

# IVUS-CHIP — Complex Lesion 定義

至少一項以下特徵：

- **Severe calcification** — 45.8% vs. 42.2%
- **Ostial lesion** — 27.5% vs. 26.6%
- **True bifurcation** (SB ≥2.5 mm) — 33.1% vs. 32.2%
- **Left main lesion** — 19.8% vs. 17.7%
- **Chronic total occlusion** — 22.0% vs. 22.2%
- **In-stent restenosis** — 16.7% vs. 18.6%
- **Long lesion** (>28 mm) — 61.7% vs. 60.2%
- Planned mechanical circulatory support

> 涵蓋 full spectrum of complex PCI！

---

# IVUS-CHIP — Procedural Data

| 特徵 | IVUS (N=999) | Angio (N=1002) |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| IVUS at any time | **98.7%** | 0.9% |
| IVUS-triggered optimization | 40.5% | — |
| Post-stent balloon dilation | **91.3%** | 84.5% |
| Stents/patient | 2.35 | 2.19 |
| Total stent length (mm) | 64.0 | 59.6 |
| Max stent diameter (mm) | **3.50** | **3.38** |
| **Procedure time (min)** | **88.8** | **66.2** |
| Contrast (ml) | 185 | 176 |
| Complications | 11.3% | 10.2% |

> IVUS-guided: 較大 stent diameter, 較長 stent, 更多 post-dilation

---

# IVUS-CHIP — Stent Optimization 分析

- 956 final IVUS pullbacks 可分析
- 僅 **53.3%** 達到 prespecified stent optimization criteria
- Lesion level: **48.0%** 達標

### 最常見未達標原因
- **Proximal/distal stent edge plaque burden >50%**

### 對比 RENOVATE-COMPLEX-PCI
- RENOVATE: 55.5% 達標
- IVUS-CHIP 族群 calcification 更嚴重（42% vs. 14%）
- 重度鈣化可能限制了 IVUS 指導下的 stent optimization

---

# IVUS-CHIP — 主要結果

## Primary Endpoint: TVF at median 19 months

| 終點 | IVUS | Angio | HR (95% CI) |
|------|------|-------|-------------|
| **Primary TVF** | **13.9%** | **11.1%** | **1.25 (0.97–1.60), P=0.08** |
| TV-MI or CI-TVR | — | — | 1.22 (0.92–1.62) |
| Cardiac death or TV-MI | — | — | 1.22 (0.87–1.71) |
| TLF | — | — | 1.11 (0.80–1.54) |
| All-cause death | — | — | 1.05 (0.75–1.48) |
| **Definite stent thrombosis** | **0.2%** | **1.0%** | **0.20 (0.04–0.90)** |

> **Stent thrombosis**: IVUS 顯著降低（HR 0.20）！但 primary endpoint 未達顯著差異

# 兩試驗交叉比較

---

# 研究設計比較

| 項目 | OPTIMAL | IVUS-CHIP |
|------|---------|-----------|
| 目標族群 | **Left main only** | **All complex PCI** |
| 樣本數 | 806 | 2019 |
| 中心數 | 28 | 37 |
| Primary endpoint | POCE | TVF |
| Follow-up | **2.9 years** | **19 months** |
| Event-driven | No | Yes (169 events) |
| Power assumption | HR 0.65 | HR 0.65 |
| Stent | Synergy | Synergy |
| DSMB | No | Yes |
| Mean SYNTAX | **29.7** | **25** |

---

# 結果比較

| 終點 | OPTIMAL | IVUS-CHIP |
|------|---------|-----------|
| **Primary endpoint** | HR 1.11 (0.87–1.42) | HR 1.25 (0.97–1.60) |
| | P = 0.40 | P = 0.08 |
| All-cause death | HR 1.06 | HR 1.05 |
| Definite stent thrombosis | HR 3.07 (0.32–29.53) | **HR 0.20 (0.04–0.90)** |
| Procedure time difference | **+24.7 min** | **+22.6 min** |
| Angio group crossover | 3.0% | 0.9% |

> **兩項試驗均為 Neutral** — IVUS-guided PCI 未優於 angiography-guided PCI

# 臨床意義

---

# 為何結果與先前試驗不同？

### 1. 操作者經驗效應
- 參與中心在試驗前已在 50-100% left main PCI 使用 IVUS
- **IVUS 知識溢出**：即使 angio-guided 也運用 IVUS 原則

### 2. Contemporary Procedural Standards
- Post-stent dilation: 84-97%
- POT: >85%

### 3. Contemporary DES Platform
- Thin-strut Synergy stent 降低 baseline event rate

### 4. 族群差異
- 歐洲 vs. 韓國（RENOVATE）
- 更老、共病更多、鈣化更嚴重

---

# IVUS 的價值 — Stent Thrombosis

## IVUS-CHIP: Definite Stent Thrombosis

| 項目 | IVUS | Angio |
|------|------|-------|
| Definite ST | 0.2% | 1.0% |
| HR (95% CI) | **0.20 (0.04–0.90)** | |

- **ILUMIEN IV** (OCT): 類似發現 — imaging 降低 stent thrombosis
- 三項試驗一致：**Intracoronary imaging 可確保 stent expansion / apposition**
- 儘管 overall MACE neutral，**stent thrombosis 降低具有臨床意義**

> **Pearl**: Intracoronary imaging 的 mechanistic value 仍然存在 — adequate expansion and apposition

---

# 實務建議

### 高量中心 + 高經驗術者
- Angiography-guided PCI + 嚴格 lesion prep + post-dilation + POT **可能已足夠**
- Routine IVUS 的 incremental benefit 有限

### 低量中心 / 低經驗術者
- **IVUS guidance 仍建議使用**
- 學習階段：IVUS 幫助建立 stent sizing 與 optimization 的臨床直覺

### 特殊情境 — IVUS 仍有價值
- Stent thrombosis 高風險：bifurcation, long stent, underexpansion
- Ambiguous angiographic result
- Lesion assessment（鈣化程度、vessel sizing）
- Complication detection
